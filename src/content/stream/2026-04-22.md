Added a Refine Pass to Prism — each analysis stage runs twice, with round 2 using round 1's output to self-critique.

Scores dropped across the board: execution 9.5→8.9, communication 8.9→8.1. But the descriptions got sharper.

Maybe that's what self-knowledge actually looks like: the more you examine, the more conservative, the more accurate. High scores feel good. Accurate scores are useful.
