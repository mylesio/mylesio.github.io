## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

队友和对方球员发生冲突，教练走过来说："Why don't you just shake hands?" 这句话是什么意思？今天我们系统学"提建议"的全套句型！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 一、提建议的六大句型

| 句型 | 例句 | 语气 |
|------|------|------|
| **Why don't you** + V? | Why don't you talk to her? | 建议（较轻松）|
| **Why not** + V? | Why not ask for help? | 建议（更简短）|
| **You should** + V | You should apologize. | 建议/应该 |
| **How about** + V-ing? | How about calling her first? | 建议 |
| **What about** + V-ing? | What about writing a letter? | 建议 |
| **Maybe you could** + V | Maybe you could talk to your parents. | 委婉建议 |

> 💡 **Why don't you...? = Why not...?**  
> 两个意思相同，Why not 更简洁，Why don't you 更常见于对话。

---

### 二、Why don't you...? 句型详解

```
结构：Why don't you + 动词原形 + ...?

例句：
Why don't you talk to your parents?        你为什么不和父母谈谈呢？
Why don't you join the school team?        你为什么不加入校队呢？
Why don't you take a break?                你为什么不休息一下呢？
```

**回应方式：**
```
✅ 接受建议：
- That's a great idea!  
- OK, I'll try that.  
- You're right. I'll do that.

✅ 拒绝建议（给理由）：
- I don't think that will work because...  
- I've already tried that.
- That's a good point, but...
```

---

### 三、should 与 Why don't you 对比

| | should | Why don't you |
|-|--------|----------------|
| 语气 | 较强（应该）| 较轻（建议）|
| 结构 | should + V原 | Why don't you + V原 |
| 例句 | You **should** rest. | **Why don't you** rest? |

---

### 四、连词 both/either/neither（搭配建议）

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| both A and B（两者都）| **Both** you **and** your friend should talk. |
| either A or B（两者之一）| You can try **either** talking **or** writing. |
| neither A nor B（两者都不）| **Neither** arguing **nor** ignoring helps. |

---

### 五、综合对话练习

```
A: I always argue with my parents about screen time.
B: Why don't you talk to them calmly about it?
A: I've tried, but they don't listen.
B: Maybe you could write them a note. How about that?
A: That's a great idea! I'll try it tonight.
```

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **Why don't you** 后接**动词原形**（不加 to）  
   ✅ Why don't you **talk**?  ❌ Why don't you **to talk**?

2. **Why not** 后也接**动词原形**  
   ✅ Why not **ask** for help?  ❌ Why not **to ask**?

3. **How about / What about** 后接 **V-ing**  
   ✅ How about **talking** to her?  ❌ How about **talk**?

4. **both A and B 谓语用复数**  
   ✅ **Both** you **and** I **are** wrong.  ❌ Both you and I **is** wrong.

5. **neither...nor... 谓语就近原则**  
   ✅ Neither she **nor** I **am** right.（动词随 I 变）
