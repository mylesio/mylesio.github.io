## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

**Amazing Records on Earth**

Our Earth is full of amazing things. From the deepest oceans to the highest mountains, there are so many natural records that make scientists and explorers excited.

The **highest mountain** in the world is Mount Qomolangma (also called Mount Everest). It is about **8,849 meters** above sea level. It is on the border between China and Nepal in Asia. Thousands of climbers have tried to reach the top, but it remains one of the most challenging goals in the world.

The **longest river** depends on how you measure it. Many people say the Nile River in Africa, which is about 6,650 kilometers long, is the longest. But some scientists believe the Amazon River in South America is longer when you include all its branches.

The **deepest lake** is Lake Baikal in Russia. It is nearly **1,642 meters** deep. It contains about 20% of the world's fresh water. The lake is so old — about 25 million years — that it is called the "oldest lake" as well.

The **driest place** on Earth is the Atacama Desert in South America. In some parts of this desert, it **almost never rains**. Scientists have found that some areas have not had rain in over 400 years!

These records remind us how **incredibly diverse** our planet is. And behind every record, there are scientists, explorers, and ordinary people who work hard to discover more about our wonderful world.

**B. 判断正误（T/F）**

1. Mount Everest is on the border between China and Japan. ___
2. Scientists agree that the Nile is definitely longer than the Amazon. ___
3. Lake Baikal is the oldest and deepest lake mentioned in the text. ___
4. The Atacama Desert is in Africa. ___

**答案：** 1. F（China and Nepal）　2. F（some scientists disagree）　3. T　4. F（South America）

**C. 单项选择**

1. What does "8,849 meters above sea level" mean?

   A. The mountain is 8,849 meters from the center of the Earth.  
   B. The mountain is 8,849 meters higher than the sea surface.  
   C. It takes 8,849 minutes to climb the mountain.  
   D. The mountain is 8,849 kilometers long.  

   > **答案：B**

2. Why do some scientists think the Amazon might be longer than the Nile?

   A. Because the Amazon has more water.  
   B. Because scientists measured them in different ways.  
   C. Because when you include its branches, the Amazon might be longer.

   > **答案：C**（第三段）

3. What is special about Lake Baikal?

   A. It is the longest lake in the world.  
   B. It is both the deepest and the oldest lake.  
   C. It has the most fish in the world.  
   D. It is the largest lake by area.  

   > **答案：B**

## 💡 易错点

### above sea level（海拔）

```
above sea level = 在海平面以上（表示海拔）
例：Mount Everest is 8,849 meters above sea level.
不要说：8,849 meters high above（结构不对）
正确：8,849 meters high / 8,849 meters above sea level
```

### contain vs include

- **contain**：包含（某物在里面）Lake Baikal **contains** 20% of fresh water.
- **include**：包括（列举时）Please **include** your name in the form.
