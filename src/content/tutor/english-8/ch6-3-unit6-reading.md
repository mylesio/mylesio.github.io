## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

新年到了，你有什么计划？排球队的同学们各有志向——有人要当工程师，有人要当运动员！  
今天我们来读一篇关于中学生新年计划的短文，练习阅读理解，直击中考题型！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 📖 阅读短文

**My New Year's Resolutions**

Hello! My name is Li Ming. I'm a student at Zhengzhou No.1 Middle School. Every year, I make New Year's resolutions. Here are my plans for next year.

First, I am going to study harder. My favorite subject is computer science, and I'm going to spend more time on it. I want to be a programmer when I grow up. I'm also going to read at least two English books every month to improve my English.

Second, I am going to get healthier. I love playing volleyball, and I'm going to join the school volleyball team. Our coach says that practice makes perfect, so I'm going to practice every day after school. I'm also going to eat more vegetables and less junk food.

Third, I am going to help my parents more. My mom works very hard, so I'm going to cook dinner on weekends. My dad is a doctor, and I'm going to learn more about medicine from him.

I know it is not easy to keep all these resolutions, but I'm going to try my best. What about you? What are your plans for next year?

---

### 🔍 阅读理解题

**根据短文内容，选择正确答案：**

**1. What school does Li Ming go to?**  
A. Beijing No.1 Middle School  
B. Zhengzhou No.1 Middle School  
C. Shanghai No.1 Middle School  
D. Henan No.1 Middle School

**答案：B**  
解析：第一段明确说 "I'm a student at Zhengzhou No.1 Middle School."

**2. What does Li Ming want to be when he grows up?**  
A. A doctor  
B. A volleyball player  
C. A programmer  
D. A teacher

**答案：C**  
解析：第二段说 "I want to be a programmer when I grow up."

**3. How often is Li Ming going to read English books?**  
A. Once a week  
B. At least two books a month  
C. Every day  
D. Once a year

**答案：B**  
解析：第二段说 "I'm going to read at least two English books every month."

**4. Why is Li Ming going to cook dinner on weekends?**  
A. Because he likes cooking  
B. Because his mom works very hard  
C. Because his dad is a doctor  
D. Because he wants to save money

**答案：B**  
解析：第四段说 "My mom works very hard, so I'm going to cook dinner on weekends."

### ✍️ 语言点归纳

**文中出现的 be going to 结构：**
1. I **am going to** study harder.（计划学习）
2. I'**m going to** spend more time on it.（计划花时间）
3. I'**m going to** join the school volleyball team.（计划加入球队）
4. I'**m going to** practice every day.（计划练习）
5. I'**m going to** cook dinner on weekends.（计划做饭）

**高频短语复习：**
- make a resolution 下定决心
- improve one's English 提高英语
- practice makes perfect 熟能生巧
- try one's best 尽全力

## 💡 易错点

### ❌ 错误1：时间状语位置
- ✅ I am going to read books **every day**.
- ✅ **Every day**, I am going to read books.（可移到句首）
- 注意：every day 是频率副词，可在句末也可在句首

### ❌ 错误2：at least 的用法
- at least = 至少（后接数字）
- at most = 最多（后接数字）
- ✅ I'm going to read **at least two** books a month.

### ❌ 错误3：spend time on sth. / spend time doing sth.
- ✅ spend time **on** computer science（on + 名词）
- ✅ spend time **doing** sth.（doing + 动词）
- ❌ spend time **to do** sth.（❌ 错误搭配）

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**完型填空节选（河南2023年中考风格）：**

Wang Fang is a student in Grade 8. She has big dreams. She __(1)__ going to be a scientist when she grows up. She is going to __(2)__ harder at school and read more books. She also plans to improve her __(3)__ by joining the school volleyball team. She knows that __(4)__ makes perfect. She is not going to __(5)__ too much time playing games.

1. A. am　　B. is　　C. are　　D. be  
2. A. study　　B. studied　　C. studies　　D. studying  
3. A. height　　B. health　　C. wealth　　D. help  
4. A. study　　B. exercise　　C. practice　　D. hard work  
5. A. spend　　B. cost　　C. take　　D. pay

**答案：1-B　2-A　3-B　4-C　5-A**

**解析：**
1. 主语 She，be going to 中 be 用 is
2. be going to 后接动词原形 study
3. 加入排球队是为了提高健康（health）
4. "熟能生巧" practice makes perfect 固定短语
5. spend time doing sth. 花时间做某事，主语是人用 spend
