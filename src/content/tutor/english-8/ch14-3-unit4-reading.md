## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

你有没有过这样的烦恼：父母不让你打球、朋友突然冷落你……今天我们读一篇"烦恼信箱"类文章，这是中考阅读的超高频题型！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

> **Dear Amy: Teen Advice Column**
>
> **Letter 1**
> Dear Amy,
> I'm 14 and I love playing volleyball. But my parents think I spend too much time training and not enough time studying. They compare me to my cousin who gets top grades. I feel very stressed and unhappy. What should I do?
> — Stressed in Zhengzhou
>
> **Amy's Reply:**
> Dear Stressed,
> I understand how you feel. Why don't you sit down with your parents and calmly explain how important volleyball is to you? You could show them your school grades too. Maybe you could make a weekly schedule that balances both training and studying. Communication is key. Remember, your parents compare you because they care about you.
>
> **Letter 2**
> Dear Amy,
> My best friend suddenly stopped talking to me last week. I don't know what I did wrong. I feel really hurt and lonely. Should I just ignore her?
> — Confused in Kaifeng
>
> **Amy's Reply:**
> Dear Confused,
> Don't ignore her — that will only make things worse! Why not write her a short message or note? Ask her gently if something is wrong. Maybe she's going through a hard time herself. True friends work things out instead of giving up on each other.

---

### 词汇注释

| 单词/短语 | 意思 |
|-----------|------|
| advice column | 答疑专栏 |
| stressed | 压力大的 |
| calmly | 平静地 |
| weekly schedule | 每周计划表 |
| balance | 平衡 |
| ignore | 忽视；不理睬 |
| gently | 温和地 |
| go through | 经历（困难）|
| work things out | 解决问题 |
| give up on | 放弃（某人）|

---

### 阅读理解

**回答问题：**

1. What is the first writer's problem?

2. What does Amy suggest the first writer do? (List 2 suggestions)

3. Why does Amy say the parents compare their child to others?

4. What does Amy advise the second writer NOT to do?

5. What does "work things out" mean in the passage?

---

### 参考答案

1. Her parents think she spends too much time on volleyball and not enough on studying.

2. ① Sit down with parents and explain calmly. ② Make a weekly schedule that balances training and studying.（任答两点即可）

3. Because they care about her.

4. Ignore her friend.

5. 解决问题（Solve the problem / find a solution together）.

---

### 仿写练习（中考书信格式）

```
Dear Amy,
I have a problem with _____.
I feel _____ because _____.
What should I do?
— [署名]

Dear [署名],
I understand how you feel.
Why don't you _____?
You could also _____.
I hope things get better soon!
— Amy
```

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **ignore vs. avoid**  
   - ignore = 故意不理睬  
   - avoid = 主动避开  
   ✅ She **ignored** my message.（不理我的消息）  
   ✅ He **avoided** the question.（回避问题）

2. **go through** 经历（不好的事情）  
   ✅ She's **going through** a hard time.（她正经历艰难时期）

3. **instead of + V-ing**  
   ✅ Work things out **instead of** giving **up**.  
   ❌ instead of give up

4. **make + 名词**（固定搭配）  
   make a **schedule** / make a **plan** / make a **decision**

5. **communicate 通讯；沟通** → Communication **is** key.（主语单数，谓语单数）
