## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

"凭什么我做所有家务？！" 这个话题在家里吵过吗？今天的文章就是一场关于家务公平分工的讨论，读完你就有论据了！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

> **Should Kids Do Housework?**
>
> Many parents and kids argue about housework. Some parents believe children should help at home because it teaches them to be responsible. Others think kids are too busy with schoolwork to do chores.
>
> Lin Mei, 14, says: "I think it's fair to share the housework. Could you please tell my parents that? Every evening, I sweep the floor and do the dishes. My brother folds the clothes. We work as a team, just like in volleyball!"
>
> Tom's mom, however, has a different view. "My son is preparing for exams. I don't want him to worry about chores. I'd rather he focused on studying."
>
> A school survey shows that 72% of students in China do some housework every week. Most students say doing chores makes them more independent and organized.
>
> Experts suggest that children should do age-appropriate chores. Young children can make their beds and tidy their rooms. Older kids can help cook, do the laundry, or take out the trash. This way, housework becomes a family activity, not just a burden.

---

### 阅读理解题

**选择正确答案：**

1. Why does Lin Mei think it's fair to share housework?  
   A. Because her parents are tired.  
   B. Because it's like working as a team.  
   C. Because she has no homework.  
   D. Because her brother doesn't help.

2. What does Tom's mom want Tom to focus on?  
   A. Sports.  B. Housework.  C. Studying.  D. Friends.

3. According to the survey, how many students do housework every week?  
   A. 52%  B. 62%  C. 72%  D. 82%

4. What does the expert suggest young children can do?  
   A. Cook meals.  B. Do the laundry.  C. Make their beds.  D. Take out the trash.

**参考答案：** 1-B  2-C  3-C  4-C

### 表达观点（写作练习）

用下列句型表达你对"中学生做家务"的看法：

```
I think / I believe + 观点
Because...
For example,...
Therefore,...

范例：
I believe students should do some housework.
Because it helps us become more responsible and independent.
For example, I sweep the floor and fold clothes every day.
Therefore, doing chores is not a waste of time — it's a life skill!
```

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **argue about vs. argue with**  
   - argue about sth. = 为某事争论（内容）  
   - argue with sb. = 与某人争论（对象）  
   ✅ We argued **about** the chores.  ✅ She argued **with** her mom.

2. **I'd rather + 动词原形**（宁愿做某事）  
   I'd rather **stay** home than go out.  
   → 中考进阶用法，了解即可

3. **as a team = 作为一个团队**，固定短语，work as a team 很常用

4. **survey 调查** → conduct a survey / do a survey  
   show 在这里是"表明"：The survey **shows** that...

5. **burden** 负担，读音 /ˈbɜːrdən/，注意 u 在这里不读 /ʌ/
