## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

你有没有因为一本书而改变了自己的想法？就像一次完美的扣球改变了比赛走向——一本好书也能改变人的一生！今天的阅读练习聚焦**现在完成时在阅读情境中的应用**，同时训练中考阅读核心题型。

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

**Books That Changed My Life**

My name is Lily, and I am fourteen years old. I love reading almost as much as I love playing volleyball. Over the past few years, I **have read** more than fifty books — adventure stories, science books, and everything in between.

The book that has **influenced** me the most is "The Old Man and the Sea" by Ernest Hemingway. I **have read** it twice already, and every time I read it, I discover something new. The story is about an old fisherman who **has struggled** with the sea for days without catching anything. But he never gives up. His spirit reminds me of our volleyball team captain — she **has never given up** in a match, no matter how far behind we are.

Another book I really enjoy is "Treasure Island" by Robert Louis Stevenson. Have you **ever** read it? I first read it two years ago, and I **have recommended** it to all my teammates since then. The story is full of excitement and adventure. Some of my teammates **have already** started reading it!

Reading has made me a better person. I **have learned** so much from books — how to be brave, how to stay calm under pressure, and how to never give up. Just like training for volleyball, reading every day **has made** me stronger.

If you **haven't** tried reading English books yet, I strongly suggest you start. You won't regret it!

### 中考题型训练

**A. 根据短文内容回答问题**

1. How many books has Lily read over the past few years?
   > **答案**：She has read more than fifty books.

2. Which book has influenced Lily the most?
   > **答案**：*The Old Man and the Sea* by Ernest Hemingway.

3. How many times has Lily read *The Old Man and the Sea*?
   > **答案**：She has read it twice.

4. Why does Lily recommend *Treasure Island* to her teammates?
   > **答案**：Because the story is full of excitement and adventure.

---

**B. 判断正误（T/F）**

1. Lily likes reading more than volleyball. ___
2. The Old Man and the Sea is a story about a fisherman who never gives up. ___
3. Lily read Treasure Island for the first time two years ago. ___
4. All of Lily's teammates have finished reading Treasure Island. ___

**答案：** 1. F（almost as much as，一样喜欢）　2. T　3. T　4. F（some have started, not finished）

## 💡 易错点

### 完成时 + 次数表达

```
once = 一次
twice = 两次
three times = 三次（three times 及以上用 times）

I've read it twice. ✅
I've read it two times. ✅（正式写作少用）
I've read it two times ago. ❌（ago 不与完成时连用）
```

### recommend 的用法

```
recommend + 名词/代词（推荐某物）
recommend + doing（建议做某事）
recommend that + 从句

I recommend this book. ✅
I recommend reading this book. ✅
❌ I recommend you to read this book.（错误，不加 to）
✅ I recommend that you read this book.
```

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**完形填空练习（节选）：**

I love reading. I __(1)__ already read more than twenty English books this year. The best one __(2)__ I have ever read is "Little Women." I __(3)__ it to all my friends, and some of them __(4)__ started reading it. Have you __(5)__ read an English novel?

| 空 | 选项 | 答案 |
|----|------|------|
| (1) | A.am B.have C.had D.was | B |
| (2) | A.what B.which C.who D.where | B |
| (3) | A.suggest B.tell C.recommended D.asked | C |
| (4) | A.have B.has C.had D.are | A |
| (5) | A.never B.just C.ever D.already | C |

The fisherman in the story never gives up, and his spirit has taught me to keep fighting even when I'm losing a volleyball match. Since I read it, I have become more determined and confident. I recommend it to everyone!
