## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

"你想做什么？""你打算怎么做？" 在英语里表达**愿望和计划**，有好几个结构经常被搞混！want to? would like to? hope to? plan to?——今天一次性全搞清楚，中考不再丢分！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 核心句型总览

| 结构 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **want to do** | 想要做某事 | I **want to watch** the game show. |
| **want sb. to do** | 想让某人做 | She **wants me to practice** more. |
| **would like to do** | 想要做（更礼貌） | I **would like to join** the team. |
| **would like sb. to do** | 想让某人做（礼貌） | I **would like you to come** to the match. |
| **hope to do** | 希望做（只能+自己做的事） | I **hope to become** a better player. |
| **hope that + 从句** | 希望… | I **hope that** we will win. |
| **plan to do** | 打算做 | We **plan to train** every day. |
| **decide to do** | 决定做 | She **decided to quit** the team. |

---

### want vs would like 对比

| | want | would like |
|-|------|------------|
| 语气 | 直接 | 礼貌、委婉 |
| 疑问句 | Do you want to...? | **Would** you **like** to...? |
| 否定句 | don't want to | **wouldn't like** to |
| 否定回答 | No, I don't. | No, **I wouldn't**. / No, **thanks**. |

---

### hope 的重要限制

**hope to do = 主语自己的愿望**  
✅ I hope to win the match.（我自己想赢）

**hope sb. to do ❌（不存在此结构！）**  
❌ I hope you to come.  
✅ I hope **(that) you will come**. / I **want** you to come.

---

### 情态动词 can / could / may / might

| 情态动词 | 主要用法 | 例句 |
|---------|---------|------|
| **can** | 能力；许可（非正式） | She **can** serve really fast. |
| **could** | can的过去式；礼貌请求 | **Could** you help me with my serve? |
| **may** | 许可（正式）；可能 | **May** I use your volleyball? |
| **might** | 较小的可能性 | We **might** win if we try hard. |

---

### 宾语从句语序（that/if/wh-）

```
主句 + that/if/whether + 从句（陈述语序！）
I wonder if the game show is on tonight.
She asked where the volleyball court was.
He said that he wanted to be a professional player.
```

> ⚠️ **宾语从句必须用陈述语序！**（把疑问句的倒装变回正常顺序）

---

## 💡 易错点

### 错误 1：hope + sb. to do

❌ I hope you to win the match.  
✅ I hope **(that) you will win** the match.  
✅ I **want** you to win the match.

### 错误 2：would like 的疑问句

❌ **Do** you would like to come?  
✅ **Would** you like to come?

### 错误 3：宾语从句语序

❌ She asked me **where was** the volleyball court.（疑问语序）  
✅ She asked me **where the volleyball court was**.（陈述语序）

### 错误 4：情态动词后的动词形式

❌ She can **plays** volleyball.  
✅ She can **play** volleyball.

❌ Would you like **going**?  
✅ Would you like **to go**?（would like 后接不定式，不是动名词）

### 错误 5：could 做礼貌请求时的回答

❌ — Could you pass me the ball?  
— Yes, I could. ❌

✅ — Could you pass me the ball?  
— **Sure! / Of course! / Sorry, I can't.**（不用 could 回答礼貌请求）

---

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（改错）：** My coach hopes me to practice serving every day.

> 📝 **答案**：hopes me to practice → **hopes that I will practice** / **wants me to practice**  
> **解析**：hope 不接"hope sb. to do"结构，需改为 hope that 从句，或换成 want sb. to do。

**题2（翻译）：** 我计划下赛季成为一名更好的排球运动员，希望我们队能赢得省冠军。

> 📝 **参考答案**：I plan to become a better volleyball player next season. I hope that our team will win the provincial championship.
