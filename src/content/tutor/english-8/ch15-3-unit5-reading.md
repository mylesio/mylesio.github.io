## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

中考经典题型：用过去进行时描述"那一刻在做什么"。今天这篇文章就像一部微型电影，你来演女主角——一个被暴雨打断训练的排球少女！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

> **A Stormy Afternoon**
>
> Last Wednesday afternoon, our school volleyball team was practicing on the outdoor court. The sky was clear when we started. We were doing warm-up exercises and laughing — it was a perfect day for training.
>
> But around 3:30 p.m., dark clouds suddenly rolled in. While we were practicing our spikes, the wind began to blow strongly. Coach Wang looked up and said, "Stop! A rainstorm is coming!"
>
> We ran inside just in time. When we reached the gym, the rain was already pouring down heavily. Lightning flashed and thunder roared. Some younger students were crying because they were frightened.
>
> I called my mom while the storm was still going on. She was cooking dinner when I called. She was relieved to hear I was safe inside. "Stay there until it stops," she said.
>
> The rainstorm lasted about two hours. When it finally stopped, the court was flooded and several trees near the school had blown down. We helped clean up the next morning.
>
> That stormy afternoon taught me something: nature is powerful, but teamwork and caring for each other are even more powerful.

### 阅读理解

**根据短文内容，选择正确答案或填空：**

1. Where was the team practicing when the storm began?  
   A. In the gym.  B. On the outdoor court.  C. In the classroom.  D. At home.

2. What was Coach Wang doing when the clouds rolled in?  
   A. Running.  B. Looking at the sky.  C. Calling parents.  D. Cleaning the court.

3. What was the writer's mom doing when she received the call?  
   A. Watching TV.  B. Working.  C. Cooking dinner.  D. Sleeping.

4. How long did the rainstorm last?  
   A. 30 minutes.  B. One hour.  C. Two hours.  D. Three hours.

5. What lesson did the writer learn from that afternoon?

**参考答案：** 1-B  2-B  3-C  4-C  5. Nature is powerful, but teamwork and caring for each other are even more powerful.

---

### 时态综合练习

将下列句子改写为过去进行时或 when 从句：

```
1. The team / practice → when → the storm / come
   → The team was practicing when the storm came.

2. My mom / cook → when → I / call her
   → My mom was cooking when I called her.

3. While / I / run inside → the rain / start / pour
   → While I was running inside, the rain started to pour.
```

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **just in time = 刚好及时**  
   We arrived **just in time** to catch the bus.（刚好没错过）  
   ≠ on time（准时，强调不迟到）

2. **pour down = 倾盆大雨**  
   It was **pouring down**. = It was raining heavily.

3. **relieve vs. relieved**  
   - relieve 是动词  
   - relieved 是形容词（感到如释重负）  
   ✅ I felt **relieved** when I heard the good news.

4. **last 作动词 = 持续**（不是「最后」）  
   The storm **lasted** two hours. = The storm continued for two hours.

5. **blow down 被吹倒**（=blow + direction），注意 blow 不规则  
   The wind **blew down** the trees. ✅  
   Several trees **were blown down**. ✅（被动语态）
