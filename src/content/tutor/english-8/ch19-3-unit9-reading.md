## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

你们排球队参加省赛，去过好几个城市了吧？每到一个新城市，都是一次文化体验！今天读一篇关于**文化交流**和**旅行经历**的短文，重点练 have been to / have gone to 的阅读理解，同时备战中考作文！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

**My Journey Through China**

My name is Amy. I am from the UK, and I have been living in China for two years as an exchange student. I **have been to** many wonderful places in China, and each trip has taught me something new.

Last summer, I **went to** Xi'an with my host family. It was my first time there. I visited the Terracotta Warriors, which were truly amazing. I **had never seen** anything like them before. My host sister told me that millions of tourists **have visited** Xi'an because of its famous history.

I **have also been to** Chengdu, the capital of Sichuan Province. I ate hot pot for the first time — it was both delicious and very spicy! My Chinese friend said, "You **have tried** the real Sichuan food now. You **have become** a true food adventurer!" I laughed and agreed.

One place I **haven't visited** yet is Guilin in Guangxi. I **have heard** that the mountains and rivers there are the most beautiful in China. My classmate Li Hua **has gone to** Guilin this week for a family trip — I'm so envious! I hope to go there before my exchange program ends.

China is such a big and diverse country. Every province I **have visited** has surprised me. I believe that **traveling is the best way to learn** about a culture. Have you been anywhere interesting recently?

### 中考题型训练

**A. 根据短文内容回答问题**

1. How long has Amy been living in China?
   > **答案**：She has been living in China for two years.

2. What did Amy see in Xi'an?
   > **答案**：She visited the Terracotta Warriors.

3. Why hasn't Amy been to Guilin yet?
   > **答案**：She hasn't had the chance yet (her exchange program hasn't ended).

4. Where has Li Hua gone this week?
   > **答案**：Li Hua has gone to Guilin for a family trip.

---

**B. 判断正误（T/F）**

1. Amy is from the USA. ___
2. Amy visited Xi'an last summer with her host family. ___
3. Amy found Chengdu hot pot delicious but very spicy. ___
4. Li Hua has already returned from Guilin. ___
5. Amy thinks the best way to learn about a culture is through books. ___

**答案：** 1. F（UK）　2. T　3. T　4. F（has gone to = 还在那里）　5. F（traveling is the best way）

---

**C. 找出文中 have been to / have gone to 的例子，并解释含义**

| 句子 | 类型 | 含义 |
|------|------|------|
| I have been to many wonderful places | have been to | 去过（已回来）|
| I have also been to Chengdu | have been to | 去过成都（已回来）|
| Li Hua has gone to Guilin this week | have gone to | 去了桂林（还在那里）|

---

## 💡 易错点

### 从本文学语法

1. **have been to** → Amy 说自己的经历（人在这里讲故事，已经回来了）
2. **have gone to** → 说 Li Hua（她不在这里，还在桂林）

这是中考最经典的考查方式——通过语境判断是 been to 还是 gone to！

### envious vs jealous

- **envious** = 羡慕（想要别人有的东西）
- **jealous** = 嫉妒（害怕失去自己有的东西，或因别人有而不满）
- 考试多用 **envious** 表示"羡慕"

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**中考写作模拟（80词）：**

以"Places I Have Been To"为题，介绍你去过的2-3个地方，用现在完成时和一般过去时结合。

> **参考答案**：  
> I have been to several interesting places in China. Last year, I went to Luoyang with my volleyball team for a competition. I visited the Longmen Grottoes and was amazed by the carvings. I have also been to Zhengzhou many times — it's my favorite city for shopping!  
> One place I haven't visited yet is the Yellow River in Sanmenxia. My coach has gone there this week, and she said it's beautiful. I hope to visit it someday.

---

**选择题综合练习：**

1. — Where is Xiao Li? — She ________ the supermarket. She'll be back in an hour.

   > A. has been to　B. has gone to　C. went to　D. will go to

   > **答案：B**（还没回来，gone to）

2. I ________ Xi'an twice. The Terracotta Warriors are unforgettable!

   > A. have gone to　B. visited　C. have been to　D. go to

   > **答案：C**（说自己的经历，已回来）
