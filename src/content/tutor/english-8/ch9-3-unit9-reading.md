## 📚 核心内容

### 📖 阅读短文（含对话）

**Planning a Surprise Party**

Li Fang is planning a surprise party for her best friend, Wang Mei, who is moving to another city next month. She is writing text messages to invite her friends.

---

**Message 1:**  
Li Fang: Hey Tom! Can you come to a surprise party for Wang Mei this Saturday at 7 pm? It's at my home.  
Tom: That sounds great! I'd love to come. Should I bring anything?  
Li Fang: Could you please bring some fruit? I'm going to prepare sandwiches and juice.  
Tom: Sure! No problem. See you on Saturday!

---

**Message 2:**  
Li Fang: Hi Amy! We're having a surprise party for Wang Mei on Saturday. Can you come?  
Amy: I'm sorry, I can't make it. I have a family trip that weekend.  
Li Fang: That's too bad. We'll miss you!  
Amy: Thank you for inviting me! Please say happy birthday to Wang Mei for me.  
Li Fang: Of course! I will.

---

**Message 3:**  
Li Fang: Jack, can you come to Wang Mei's surprise party this Saturday?  
Jack: Oh, I'd love to! But what time does it start?  
Li Fang: At 7 pm. Could you come a little early to help me prepare?  
Jack: Sure! I'll be there at 6:30.  
Li Fang: Perfect! Don't tell Wang Mei — it's a surprise!  
Jack: Don't worry, I won't!

---

### 🔍 阅读理解题

**1. Why is Li Fang planning a party for Wang Mei?**  
A. It's Wang Mei's birthday.  
B. Wang Mei is moving to another city.  
C. Wang Mei won a volleyball match.  
D. Wang Mei got good grades.

**答案：B**  
解析：第一句说 "Wang Mei, who is moving to another city next month."

**2. What is Tom going to bring to the party?**  
A. Sandwiches  
B. Juice  
C. Fruit  
D. A cake

**答案：C**  
解析：Li Fang 要求 "Could you please bring some fruit?" Tom 回答 "Sure! No problem."

**3. Why can't Amy come to the party?**  
A. She has a volleyball match.  
B. She has a headache.  
C. She has a family trip.  
D. She has an exam.

**答案：C**  
解析：Amy 说 "I have a family trip that weekend."

**4. What time will Jack arrive at the party?**  
A. 7:00 pm  
B. 6:00 pm  
C. 7:30 pm  
D. 6:30 pm

**答案：D**  
解析：Jack 说 "I'll be there at 6:30."

---

### 🔍 语言点分析

**1. 邀请句型回顾（文中出现的）：**
- Can you come to a surprise party...?（基本邀请）
- We're having a surprise party... Can you come?（变体邀请）
- Could you come a little early to help me?（礼貌请求）

**2. 接受邀请（文中）：**
- That sounds great! I'd love to come.（Tom）
- Sure! No problem.（Tom）
- I'd love to!（Jack）

**3. 拒绝邀请（文中）：**
- I'm sorry, I can't make it. I have a family trip.（Amy）
- 公式：I'm sorry, I can't. + 理由。

**4. 礼貌表达：**
- Could you please + 动词...?（礼貌请求）
- Thank you for inviting me!（感谢邀请）
- Please say... for me.（代为转达）

### ✍️ 综合运用

**情景任务：**  
你的朋友小华要给你发邀请，请你参加周五晚上7点的聚会。你有排球训练，不能去。请写一段回复消息（3-4句话）：
1. 表示遗憾不能参加
2. 说明原因
3. 祝他们玩得开心

**参考答案：**
```
Hi Xiaohua!

I'm so sorry, I can't make it on Friday evening.
I have volleyball training from 5 to 8 pm that day.
I hope everyone has a great time at the party!
Thank you for inviting me. Maybe next time!

[Your name]
```

## 💡 易错点

### ❌ 错误1：Thank you for + 动名词
- ✅ Thank you for **inviting** me!
- ❌ Thank you for **invite** me!
- 介词 for 后面接动名词（-ing）

### ❌ 错误2：I can't make it（固定表达）
- make it = 成功到达；做到
- I can't make it = 我来不了（口语中常用）
- ✅ I'm sorry, I can't make it to the party.
- 不要直译成 "I can't do it" 或 "I can't arrive"

### ❌ 错误3：Should I bring anything?（肯定句形式的任何人）
- anything 用于疑问句和否定句
- something 用于肯定句或提供/请求
- ✅ Should I bring **anything**?（疑问）
- ✅ Please bring **something** to eat.（请求/建议，期待肯定）

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**书面表达（中考写作风格）：**

你的英国朋友 Mike 邀请你参加他周六的生日派对，但你有英语考试。请给 Mike 写一封回信（4-5句话），表示不能参加，并说明原因，同时祝他生日快乐。

**参考范文：**
```
Dear Mike,

Thank you so much for inviting me to your birthday party.
I'm really sorry, but I can't come this Saturday.
I have an important English exam on Saturday morning,
and I need to study hard for it.

I hope you have a wonderful birthday party!
Happy Birthday, Mike! I'll give you a gift next time we meet.

Your friend,
[Name]
```

**关键词汇：**
- Thank you for inviting me（感谢邀请）
- I'm sorry, but I can't come（礼貌拒绝）
- I have an exam（说明原因）
- I hope you have a wonderful time（祝福）
