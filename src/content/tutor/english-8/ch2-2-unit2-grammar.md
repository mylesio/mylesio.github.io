## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

你每天都练排球吗？还是只在周末练？今天我们把**频率副词**和**一般现在时**彻底搞清楚，让你能精准描述自己的训练习惯——这在中考写作里也超级常用！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 一般现在时回顾

**用法：** 表示习惯性动作、客观事实、现在的状态

**构成：**
- 肯定句：主语 + 动词（第三人称单数加 -s/es）
- 否定句：主语 + **don't/doesn't** + 动词原形
- 疑问句：**Do/Does** + 主语 + 动词原形?

| 主语 | 助动词（否定/疑问） | 动词 |
|------|-------------------|------|
| I / you / we / they | do / don't | exercise / play... |
| he / she / it | does / doesn't | exercise / play... |

---

### 第三人称单数变化规则

| 规则 | 例词 | 变化 |
|------|------|------|
| 直接加 -s | play, eat, run | plays, eats, runs |
| 以 s/x/ch/sh/o 结尾，加 -es | watch, go, teach | watches, goes, teaches |
| 辅音+y，变 y→i 加 -es | study, try | studies, tries |

---

### 频率副词在句中的位置

**规则：**
```
① be 动词/情态动词 + 频率副词
   She is always on time.
   He can never be late.

② 频率副词 + 实义动词
   I usually exercise in the morning.
   She hardly ever eats fast food.

③ sometimes 比较灵活，可以放句首
   Sometimes I feel nervous before a game.
   I sometimes feel nervous before a game.
```

---

### 频率表达综合运用

**How often ... ?** 的回答方式：

| 回答 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| 频率副词 | I **always** practice after school. |
| 次数 + 时间 | **Twice a day / Three times a week** |
| every + 时间 | **Every morning / Every two days** |

---

### 含频率副词的完整句型对比

| 类型 | 例句 | 分析 |
|------|------|------|
| be + 频率副词 | She **is always** tired after training. | be动词后 |
| 频率副词 + 实义动词 | She **always feels** tired after training. | 实义动词前 |
| 否定句 | She **doesn't usually** eat after 9 pm. | doesn't + usually(可前可后，通常在 doesn't 后) |

---

## 💡 易错点

### 错误 1：第三人称单数助动词用 do 而非 does

❌ Does she **plays** volleyball every day?  
✅ Does she **play** volleyball every day?

> 🔑 Does 已经表示第三人称，后面动词用**原形**！

### 错误 2：频率副词位置颠倒

❌ She exercises **never** in the morning.  
✅ She **never** exercises in the morning.

❌ He **is never** happy / He never **is** happy.  
✅ He **is never** happy. （be动词后）

### 错误 3：how often 的回答用了 how long 的答法

❌ — How often do you exercise? — For one hour.  
✅ — How often do you exercise? — **Once a day.** / **Every day.**

> "For one hour" 是时长（how long 的答法），不是频率！

### 错误 4：第三人称动词变化错误

❌ She **studys** hard every day.  
✅ She **studies** hard every day.

❌ He **watchs** TV at night.  
✅ He **watches** TV at night.

---

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（完成对话）：** 
A: ________ your sister exercise?  
B: Yes, she ________. She ________ every morning.

> 📝 **答案**：Does / does / exercises  
> **解析**：your sister 是第三人称单数，疑问句用 Does，简短回答用 does，肯定句动词加 -s。

**题2（改错）：** Our coach often tells us: "A healthy lifestyle help us play better on the court."

> 📝 **答案**：help → **helps**  
> **解析**：A healthy lifestyle 是第三人称单数主语，动词需加 -s → helps。
