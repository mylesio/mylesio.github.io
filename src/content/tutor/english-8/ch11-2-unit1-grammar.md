## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

训练结束，队友小华说脚踝很痛。作为队长，你该怎么给她建议？今天学的 **should** 就是你的"建议神器"，中考必考，排球场上也用得着！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 一、should 的基本用法

**should** 是情态动词，表示"**应该；建议**"，语气比 must 弱，比 can 正式。

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| should + 动词原形 | You **should rest** after the game. 比赛后你应该休息。|
| should not (shouldn't) + 动词原形 | You **shouldn't eat** before swimming. 游泳前不应该吃东西。|
| Should + 主语 + 动词原形...? | **Should** I see a doctor? 我应该去看医生吗？|

---

### 二、What's the matter? 求建议的完整对话

```
A: What's the matter?          你怎么了？
B: I have a sore throat.       我嗓子疼。
A: You should drink more water and rest.
   你应该多喝水，好好休息。
B: Should I see a doctor?      我需要看医生吗？
A: Yes, you should. / No, you shouldn't.
```

---

### 三、should vs. must vs. can（对比）

| 情态动词 | 语气 | 意思 | 例句 |
|----------|------|------|------|
| **should** | 建议/应该 | 弱义务 | You **should** sleep early. |
| **must** | 必须 | 强义务 | You **must** hand in homework. |
| **can** | 可以 | 能力/许可 | You **can** go home now. |
| **shouldn't** | 不应该 | 弱禁止 | You **shouldn't** eat junk food. |
| **must not** | 禁止 | 强禁止 | You **must not** run in the hall. |

---

### 四、常见健康建议句型

| 情景 | 建议句 |
|------|--------|
| 发烧 | You should **drink more water** and **rest**. |
| 牙痛 | You should **see a dentist**. |
| 头痛 | You should **lie down** for a while. |
| 腿痛 | You should **stop training** and **put ice on it**. |
| 感冒 | You should **take some medicine** and **keep warm**. |

---

### 五、否定变疑问变换规律

```
陈述句：She should see a doctor.
否定句：She shouldn't see a doctor.
一般疑问：Should she see a doctor?
特殊疑问：What should she do?
```

> 💡 情态动词变疑问句，直接提前到句首，不用加 do/does！

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **should 后面必须接动词原形**  
   ✅ You should **rest**.  ❌ You should **to rest** / **resting**.

2. **否定是 shouldn't，不是 should not to**  
   ✅ You **shouldn't** eat cold food.  ❌ You **should not to** eat cold food.

3. **should 没有人称变化**（第三人称不加 s！）  
   ✅ She **should** drink water.  ❌ She **shoulds** drink water.

4. **回答 Should I...? 时**  
   ✅ Yes, you should. / No, you shouldn't.  
   ❌ Yes, you should do. / No, you shouldn't do. （不加 do）

5. **What's the matter with...?** 后接人/身体部位  
   ✅ What's the matter **with your leg**?  
   ❌ What's the matter **of your leg**?
