## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

中考作文常考"公益活动"主题，今天这篇文章就是活生生的范文！读完它，你不仅能做阅读题，还能直接套用写作文——一举两得！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

> **Our Volunteer Day**
>
> Last Sunday, students from Class 8 organized a volunteer day to help clean up Sunshine Park. Over 30 students volunteered to take part.
>
> In the morning, we divided into three groups. The first group picked up trash and cleaned the paths. The second group fixed up the broken benches. The third group gave out flyers to visitors to tell them to protect the environment.
>
> In the afternoon, we visited an old people's home nearby. Many of the old people there are lonely because their children live far away. We sang songs, played games, and read newspapers to them. Some of us will come back every month to care for them.
>
> After the volunteer day, everyone felt happy and proud. Our monitor said, "I'll help to organize more activities like this. Helping others makes me feel great!"
>
> I believe small actions can make a big difference. If everyone volunteers, our city will become a better place to live.

---

### 词汇注释

| 单词/短语 | 意思 |
|-----------|------|
| organize | 组织 |
| take part | 参加 |
| divide into | 分成 |
| pick up | 捡起 |
| path | 小路 |
| broken bench | 坏掉的长椅 |
| flyer | 传单 |
| protect | 保护 |
| monitor | 班长 |
| make a difference | 产生影响；有所不同 |

---

### 阅读理解

**判断正误（T/F）：**

1. More than 30 students took part in the volunteer day. ( )
2. The second group cleaned the paths. ( )
3. The old people's home was very far from the park. ( )
4. The students plan to visit the old people every month. ( )
5. The monitor says she will organize more activities. ( )

**参考答案：** 1. T  2. F（第一组清路，第二组修椅子）  3. F（nearby，就在附近）  4. T  5. T

---

### 写作仿写

用 will + 短语动词写一篇 50 字以内的公益活动简介：

```
例：
Next Saturday, our volleyball team will volunteer to clean up the school playground.
We will give out water to workers nearby.
I hope everyone will join us and make our school a better place!
```

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **take part (in) vs. join**  
   - take part in + 活动：take part in the volunteer day  
   - join + 组织/人：join our team / join us  
   ✅ I want to **join** the team and **take part in** the activity.

2. **make a difference** 表示"产生重要影响"（不是"制造不同"）  

3. **divide into vs. separate from**  
   - divide into = 分成若干部分  
   ✅ We divided **into** three groups.  ❌ We divided **to** three groups.

4. **feel + 形容词**（系动词 feel 后接形容词）  
   ✅ We felt **happy**.  ❌ We felt **happily**.
