## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

排球场上，谁跳得更高？谁的力量更大？比较谁更厉害，英语里全靠**形容词比较级**！今天彻底搞清楚它的构成和用法，中考必考！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 比较级构成规则

| 规则 | 例词 | 比较级 |
|------|------|--------|
| 直接加 -er | tall, fast, long | taller, faster, longer |
| 以 e 结尾，加 -r | large, nice | larger, nicer |
| 以辅音 + y 结尾，变 y → i + er | happy, easy, funny | happier, easier, funnier |
| 重读闭音节（辅元辅），双写末字母 + er | big, hot, thin | bigger, hotter, thinner |
| 多音节（3音节及以上），前加 more | important, outgoing | more important, more outgoing |
| 两音节（大多数），前加 more | careful, active | more careful, more active |
| 特殊：2音节以 -er/-ow/-le/-y 结尾 | clever, narrow | cleverer/more clever |

---

### 常见不规则比较级

| 原级 | 比较级 | 最高级（预习） |
|------|--------|--------------|
| good / well | **better** | best |
| bad / badly | **worse** | worst |
| many / much | **more** | most |
| little | **less** | least |
| far | **farther / further** | farthest / furthest |

---

### 比较级核心句型

**① A + be + 比较级 + than + B**
> She is **taller than** me. / She is **more outgoing than** her sister.

**② A + 实义动词 + 比较级 + than + B**
> She runs **faster than** me. / He trains **harder than** anyone.

**③ 否定：not as + 原级 + as**
> She is not as tall as me. = She is **shorter than** me.

**④ 修饰比较级（程度加强）**

| 修饰词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|--------|------|------|
| a little / a bit | 一点点 | She is **a little** taller. |
| much / a lot / far | 多得多 | He is **much** stronger. |
| even | 甚至更… | She is **even** better now. |

---

## 💡 易错点

### 错误 1：双写辅音规则忘记

❌ She is **biger** than me.  
✅ She is **bigger** than me.

❌ He is **thiner** than before.  
✅ He is **thinner** than before.

> 🔑 重读闭音节口诀：**辅-元-辅，双写末辅音**（big → b-i-g → 双写g）

### 错误 2：比较级前加 more + er 双重比较

❌ She is **more taller** than me.  
✅ She is **taller** than me.

❌ He is **more smarter**.  
✅ He is **smarter**.

### 错误 3：than 后代词格

❌ She is taller than **I** am. → ✅ (formal, ok)  
❌ She is taller than **me** → ✅ (informal, ok in spoken English)  
> 🇨🇳 中考规范写法：than + 代词宾格（me, him, her, us, them）

### 错误 4：比较对象不平行

❌ My height is taller than **Tom**. （高度比人，不平行）  
✅ My height is **greater** than **Tom's**. （高度比高度）  
✅ I am taller than **Tom**. （人比人）

### 错误 5：good/bad 的比较级

❌ She plays **gooder** / **more good**.  
✅ She plays **better**.

---

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（句型转换）：** My score is 90. Her score is 85. (用比较级改写)

> 📝 **答案**：My score is higher than hers. / I got a better score than her.

**题2（改错）：** She is more smarter than anyone else on the team.

> 📝 **答案**：more smarter → **smarter**（smart 是单音节，直接加 -er，不能同时用 more）
