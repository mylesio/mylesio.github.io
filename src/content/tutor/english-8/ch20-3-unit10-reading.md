## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

Unit 10 的最后一课！让我们用一篇**排球主题**的文章把整个现在完成时单元收个尾。这篇文章融合了 Unit 8-10 全部语法点：have/has done、have been to、have gone to、for、since，同时也是中考作文写作模板——**把这篇文章背下来，作文分就上去了！**

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

**My Volleyball Story**

My name is Chen Xiaomei, and I am fourteen years old. I have played volleyball since I was eleven, and it has become the most important part of my life.

When I first started, I could barely hit the ball over the net. But **since then**, everything has changed. I **have trained** for over three years, and I **have learned** so many skills — spiking, blocking, and serving. Every practice **has taught** me something new.

Our coach, Coach Li, **has coached** volleyball for more than fifteen years. She **has been to** many cities for competitions, and she brings back new ideas every time. Last week, she **went to** Guangzhou for a national coaching workshop and came back with fresh training methods. Now she **has started** a new training program for us.

Our team **has won** several trophies **since** we entered the provincial league two years ago. We **have never given up**, even when we were losing. One match in Zhengzhou will always stay in my memory — we **were** behind by ten points in the fifth set, but we **came** back and won. **Since that match**, I **have believed** that anything is possible.

I **have also been to** three different cities for competitions — Luoyang, Xinxiang, and Zhengzhou. Each city was wonderful, and I **have made** many new friends from different schools.

Some of my teammates **have already** decided to pursue volleyball as a career. Others **haven't decided** yet. As for me, I just know that I want to keep playing — **for as long as I can**.

### 中考题型训练

**A. 根据短文回答问题**

1. How long has Xiaomei played volleyball?
   > **答案**：She has played volleyball for over three years (since she was eleven).

2. Where did Coach Li go last week?
   > **答案**：She went to Guangzhou for a national coaching workshop.

3. Which cities has Xiaomei visited for competitions?
   > **答案**：She has been to Luoyang, Xinxiang, and Zhengzhou.

4. What was the most memorable match for Xiaomei? Why?
   > **答案**：The match in Zhengzhou, because they were behind by ten points in the fifth set but came back and won.

---

**B. 判断正误（T/F）**

1. Xiaomei has played volleyball since she was ten. ___
2. Coach Li has more than fifteen years of volleyball coaching experience. ___
3. Coach Li has gone to Guangzhou and hasn't returned yet. ___
4. Xiaomei's team has never won a competition. ___
5. All of Xiaomei's teammates want to become professional volleyball players. ___

**答案：** 1. F（eleven）　2. T　3. F（came back with fresh methods = 已回来）　4. F（has won several trophies）　5. F（some haven't decided yet）

---

**C. 词汇理解**

"I could barely hit the ball over the net" — what does "barely" mean here?

> **答案**：几乎不能；勉强（说明刚开始技术很差）

## 💡 易错点

### 课文语法总结

**三种情况的对比（从课文提取）：**

| 情况 | 例句 | 时态 |
|------|------|------|
| 过去经历（具体时间）| She went to Guangzhou last week. | 过去时（有 last week）|
| 持续到现在（无具体时间）| She has coached for 15 years. | 完成时（for 时间段）|
| 从某时刻开始持续 | I have played since I was 11. | 完成时（since + 从句）|

## 🎯 河南中考作文训练

**中考真题仿写（100词）：**

以"My Story with ______（填入你的爱好）"为题，仿照课文结构写一篇短文。使用：
- 现在完成时 + for/since
- 具体经历（过去时）
- have been to（去过的地方）
- 结尾表达未来展望

>  
> My coach has taught me not just volleyball skills, but also teamwork and confidence. I have become a much stronger person since I started playing.  
>
