## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

你跟队友谁跳得更高？谁的发球更快？Unit 3 全是**比较类**词汇，说"更高、更快、更强"全靠它！学会这一单元，你就能把排球场上的一切比较说得清清楚楚！

## 📚 核心内容

### 重点单词表

| 单词 | 词性 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| outgoing | adj. | 外向的，开朗的 | She is more **outgoing** than me. |
| calm | adj. | 平静的，冷静的 | A good setter must be **calm** under pressure. |
| wild | adj. | 疯狂的；野生的 | The crowd went **wild** when we scored. |
| serious | adj. | 严肃的；认真的 | Be **serious** about your training. |
| athletic | adj. | 运动型的；体育的 | She is very **athletic** and loves sports. |
| funny | adj. | 有趣的；滑稽的 | Our coach is really **funny** after games. |
| hard-working | adj. | 勤奋的 | She is the most **hard-working** player. |
| smart | adj. | 聪明的 | A **smart** player reads the game well. |
| talented | adj. | 有天赋的 | She is a very **talented** volleyball player. |
| popular | adj. | 受欢迎的 | Volleyball is very **popular** in our school. |

| hard-working | 勤奋 | lazy | 懒惰 |

### 🔑 常用搭配

| 短语 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| be similar to | 与…相似 | My style **is similar to** hers. |
| care about | 关心 | She **cares about** her teammates. |
| be good at | 擅长 | I **am good at** serving. |
| be different from | 与…不同 | His style **is different from** mine. |
| share ... with | 与…分享 | I **share** my notes **with** classmates. |

## 💡 易错点

### 1. though vs although vs however

| 词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|----|------|------|
| **although/though** | 连词，引导让步状语从句（不与 but 连用！） | **Although** she is short, she plays well. ✅ |
| **however** | 副词，句首或句中，用逗号隔开 | She is short. **However**, she plays well. ✅ |

❌ Although she is short, **but** she plays well.（although 和 but 不能连用）  
✅ **Although** she is short, she plays well.  
✅ She is short. **But** she plays well.

### 2. both 的用法

- **both A and B** = A和B都（谓语用复数）
- **Both of them** = 他们两个都

❌ Both of them **is** good players.  
✅ Both of them **are** good players.

### 3. similar to vs different from

- be similar **to** ✅（不是 similar with）  
- be different **from** ✅（不是 different to/than）

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（翻译）：** 虽然她比我矮，但她的排球技术比我好。

> 📝 **参考答案**：Although she is shorter than me, her volleyball skills are better than mine.  
> / Though she is shorter than me, she has better volleyball skills.

**题2（改错）：** Although I am tired, but I still finished the training.

> 📝 **答案**：删去 but（或改为 Although → 删去，保留 but）  
> **解析**：although 和 but 不能同时出现在同一句话中。
