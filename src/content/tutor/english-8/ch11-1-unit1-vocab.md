## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

比赛打到关键分，小美突然觉得脚踝疼、手臂酸——教练问："What's the matter?" 你能用英语描述伤情吗？今天学会这些词，上场前先把"身体词典"背熟！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 重点单词表

| 单词 | 词性 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| matter | n./v. | 问题；事情；要紧 | What's the **matter** with you? |
| sore | adj. | 疼痛的 | My throat is **sore** after shouting. |
| stomachache | n. | 胃痛；肚子痛 | I have a **stomachache**. |
| headache | n. | 头痛 | The noise gave me a **headache**. |
| fever | n. | 发烧 | She has a **fever** of 39°C. |
| toothache | n. | 牙痛 | I can't eat because of a **toothache**. |
| tired | adj. | 疲倦的 | I'm so **tired** after three sets. |
| rest | v./n. | 休息 | You should **rest** for a day. |
| medicine | n. | 药 | Take this **medicine** three times a day. |
| dentist | n. | 牙医 | Go to the **dentist** right away! |
| throat | n. | 喉咙 | My **throat** hurts when I swallow. |
| lie | v. | 躺下；说谎 | **Lie** down and get some sleep. |
| stress | n. | 压力；紧张 | Training brings a lot of **stress**. |
| knee | n. | 膝盖 | She hurt her **knee** during the jump. |
| ankle | n. | 脚踝 | I twisted my **ankle** at practice. |
| shoulder | n. | 肩膀 | My **shoulder** is stiff from serving. |
| serious | adj. | 严重的 | Is the injury **serious**? |
| advice | n. | 建议（不可数） | The doctor gave me good **advice**. |
| problem | n. | 问题 | Back pain is a common **problem**. |

---

### 易错辨析

| 易混点 | 正确用法 | 错误写法 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| stomachache | stomach**ache**（连写）| stomach ache（分开❌）|
| headache | headache（连写）| head ache（分开❌）|
| advice | advice 不可数，**an advice** ❌ | some advice ✅ |
| lie / lay | **lie down**（躺）过去式 lay | **lay down** ❌（lay 是 lie 过去式，不是祈使句）|
| sore vs. painful | sore 强调"酸痛" | painful 强调"剧痛" |

---

### 身体部位快速图

```
头部：head → 头   face → 脸   eye → 眼   ear → 耳   nose → 鼻   throat → 喉咙   tooth → 牙
上肢：shoulder → 肩   arm → 手臂   elbow → 肘   wrist → 手腕   hand → 手   finger → 手指
躯干：neck → 脖子   chest → 胸   stomach → 胃/肚子   back → 背
下肢：hip → 髋   knee → 膝盖   ankle → 脚踝   foot → 脚   toe → 脚趾
```

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **have a + ache** 句型：have a headache / stomachache / toothache ✅  
   但：have a **sore** throat（喉咙痛不说 throatache！）

2. **What's the matter (with sb.)?** → 后面接 with，不接 of  
   ✅ What's the matter **with** you?  ❌ What's the matter **of** you?

3. **should** 后面接**动词原形**：You should **rest** ✅  You should **to rest** ❌

4. **advice** 永远是不可数名词：give sb. **some advice**，不说 *an advice* 或 *advices*
