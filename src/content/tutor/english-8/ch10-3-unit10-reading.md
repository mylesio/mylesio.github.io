## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

人生处处是选择：去还是不去？练还是不练？  
"**如果**你坚持练习，**你就会**越来越好。"  
"**如果**你放弃，**你就会**后悔。"  
今天通过阅读，把 if 条件句用得滚瓜烂熟，同时练中考阅读技巧！

## 📚 核心内容

### 📖 阅读短文

**Making Good Choices**

Every day we make choices. Some choices are small, like what to eat for breakfast. Others are bigger, like whether to study hard or give up. The choices we make today will affect our future.

Imagine you are on your school volleyball team. Your team has an important match next Saturday. But your friend is also having a birthday party on the same day. What will you do?

If you go to the match, you will play for your team and your teammates will be happy. You might not win, but you will feel proud because you tried your best. Your coach will also be pleased with your commitment.

If you go to the party instead, your friend will be happy, but your teammates might feel upset. Your coach might not let you play in the next match. You may regret missing the important game later.

Of course, there is a third choice. You could talk to your friend and explain the situation. If you are honest with your friend, he or she will probably understand. A true friend will support your decision. After the match, you can celebrate together!

The most important thing is to think carefully before you make a choice. If you think about the results first, you will make better decisions. And if you always try your best, you will have no regrets.

### 🔍 阅读理解题

**1. What is the main topic of this passage?**  
A. How to play volleyball well  
B. How to have a great birthday party  
C. Making good choices and thinking about results  
D. Why sports are more important than friends

**答案：C**  
解析：文章围绕"做选择、考虑后果"展开，不是讲排球技术或派对。

**2. If a student goes to the volleyball match instead of the party, which of the following is TRUE?**  
A. The coach will be angry.  
B. The student will feel proud.  
C. The teammates will be upset.  
D. The friend will be happy.

**答案：B**  
解析：第三段说 "you will feel proud because you tried your best."

**3. What does the writer suggest as a "third choice"?**  
A. Skip both the match and the party  
B. Ask the coach for permission to leave  
C. Talk to your friend honestly and explain  
D. Let your teammates decide for you

**答案：C**  
解析：第五段说 "You could talk to your friend and explain the situation."

**4. According to the last paragraph, what is the MOST important thing?**  
A. Always going to parties  
B. Thinking carefully before making a choice  
C. Listening to your coach  
D. Having no homework

**答案：B**  
解析：最后一段 "The most important thing is to think carefully before you make a choice."

找出文中所有 if 条件句并分析：

| if 从句（原因） | 主句（结果） |
|----------------|-------------|
| If you go to the match | you will play for your team |
| If you go to the party instead | your friend will be happy / teammates might feel upset |
| If you are honest with your friend | he/she will probably understand |
| If you think about the results first | you will make better decisions |

### 🔍 词义猜测

1. **commitment** (paragraph 3): "Your coach will be pleased with your **commitment**."  
   → 意思是：**承诺；投入；责任感**（从上下文推断：教练会对你的认真态度感到满意）

2. **regret** (paragraph 4): "You may **regret** missing the important game later."  
   → 意思是：**后悔；遗憾**（错过重要比赛会后悔）

3. **honest** (paragraph 5): "If you are **honest** with your friend"  
   → 意思是：**诚实的；坦诚的**

### ✍️ 综合运用

**任务1：用 if 条件句完成下面的因果链**

情景：期末考试要到了，你在考虑是否要认真复习。

```
If you _________________, you will _________________.
If you don't _________________, you might _________________.
```

**参考答案：**
```
If you study hard every day, you will get good grades.
If you don't review your notes, you might fail the exam.
```

**任务2：书面表达**

你的朋友小亮打算在期末考试前一天去打游戏，不复习。请给他写一段建议（3-4句，用if条件句）。

**参考答案：**
```
If you play games all day, you won't have time to review.
If you don't study, you might fail the exam.
But if you study hard tonight, you will feel confident tomorrow.
Make the right choice — you will not regret it!
```

## 💡 易错点

### ❌ 错误1：if 从句时态
- 写文章时最容易犯的错：在 if 从句里用了 will
- ✅ If you **are** honest, your friend will understand.
- ❌ If you **will be** honest, your friend will understand.

### ❌ 错误2：regret 的搭配
- regret **doing** sth. = 后悔做了某事（已经做了）
- regret **to do** sth. = 遗憾地做某事（将要做，通知坏消息时）
- ✅ I regret **missing** the party.（后悔错过了派对）
- ✅ I regret **to tell** you that you didn't pass.（遗憾地告诉你……）

### ❌ 错误3：affect vs. effect
- affect = 影响（动词）
- effect = 影响（名词）
- ✅ Choices **affect** your future.（动词）
- ✅ Choices have a great **effect** on your future.（名词）

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**完型填空（河南中考风格）：**

Making choices is not always easy. If you ①(choose) the wrong path, you might ②(regret) it later. But if you think ③(careful) and ask for ④(advise), you will make a better decision. Remember: every choice has a ⑤(result).

**答案（注意形式变化）：**
1. **choose**（if 从句用一般现在时）
2. **regret**（might + 动词原形）
3. **carefully**（修饰动词 think，用副词）
4. **advice**（advice 是不可数名词，不加 s！）
5. **result**（名词，有冠词 a）

---

**书面表达（中考标准题型）：**

题目：*If I work hard this semester...*（如果这学期我努力的话）

要求：4-6句，用if条件句，描述努力学习的好结果。

**参考范文：**
```
If I work hard this semester, I will improve all my grades.
If I practice English every day, I will speak more fluently.
If I join the volleyball team and train seriously, I will become a better player.
My parents will be proud of me if I do well in my exams.
I know that if I never give up, I will achieve my goals.
Let's make the right choices and work hard together!
```
