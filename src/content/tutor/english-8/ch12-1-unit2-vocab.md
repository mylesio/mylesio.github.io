## 💡 易错点

1. **volunteer 作动词**：volunteer to do sth.（自愿做某事）  
   ✅ She **volunteered to help** clean the park.  
   ❌ She volunteered **helping** the park.

2. **give out vs. give up vs. give away**  
   - give out = 分发  
   - give up = 放弃  
   - give away = 赠送/泄露

3. **put off + doing**（不接不定式）  
   ✅ Don't put off **doing** your homework.  ❌ Don't put off **to do**.

4. **run out of** 主语是人/物，**run out** 主语是物  
   ✅ We **ran out of** water.（我们把水用完了）  
   ✅ The water **ran out**.（水用完了）

5. **lonely vs. alone**  
   - lonely = 孤独的（感情上）  
   - alone = 独自的（状态上）  
   She lives **alone** but doesn't feel **lonely**.
