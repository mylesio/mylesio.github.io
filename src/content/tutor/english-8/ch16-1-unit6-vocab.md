## 💡 易错点

### 1. try 的两种搭配

```
try to do = 努力/尽力做（可能没做到）
try doing = 尝试做（换个方法试试）

The old man tried to move the mountain. ✅（努力想移山）
Try practicing three hours a day. ✅（试着每天练三小时）
```

### 2. instead vs instead of

```
instead = 副词，单独用，常在句尾/句首
instead of = 介词短语，后接名词/动名词

He didn't give up. He worked harder instead. ✅
Instead of giving up, he worked harder. ✅
❌ Instead of, he worked harder.
```

### 3. 形容词对比

| 词 | 反义词 | 例句 |
|----|--------|------|
| wise（明智的） | silly/foolish（愚蠢的） | Is he wise or silly? |
| possible（可能的） | impossible（不可能的） | Nothing is impossible. |
| whole（整个的） | part（部分的） | the whole team |

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（单选）：** The girl ________ to become a professional volleyball player, so she trains five hours every day.

> A. is determined　B. determined　C. was determined　D. determines

> 📝 **答案：A**（is determined to = 下定决心做某事，现在时态符合语境）

**题2（翻译）：** 不要嘲笑别人的梦想，因为没有什么是不可能的。

> 📝 **参考答案**：Don't laugh at others' dreams, because nothing is impossible.
