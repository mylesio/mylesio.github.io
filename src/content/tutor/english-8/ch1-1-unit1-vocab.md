## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

暑假集训结束了！教练问你："Where did you go on vacation?" 你能用英语答上来吗？今天我们学 Unit 1 的核心词汇，让你把假期故事说得又快又准！

## 📚 核心内容

### 重点单词表

| 单词 | 词性 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| vacation | n. | 假期 | I had a great **vacation** in Beijing. |
| spend | v. | 度过；花费 | We **spent** three days at the beach. |
| seem | v. | 似乎，好像 | She **seems** happy after the trip. |
| anything | pron. | 任何事物（否定/疑问） | Did you do **anything** special? |
| nothing | pron. | 没有什么 | I did **nothing** but sleep. |
| everyone | pron. | 每个人 | **Everyone** enjoyed the game. |
| together | adv. | 一起 | We played volleyball **together**. |
| most | adj./adv. | 最多；大多数 | I liked the beach **most**. |
| feel | v. | 感觉；摸起来 | I **felt** tired after training. |
| enough | adj./adv. | 足够的 | We didn't have **enough** time. |
| yourself | pron. | 你自己 | Did you enjoy **yourself**? |
| few | adj. | 少数的（可数） | Only a **few** students came. |
| quite | adv. | 相当；完全 | The beach was **quite** beautiful. |
| decide | v. | 决定 | We **decided** to go to Luoyang. |
| try | v. | 尝试；努力 | I **tried** local food in Zhengzhou. |
| delicious | adj. | 美味的 | The noodles were **delicious**! |
| diary | n. | 日记 | I wrote a **diary** every day. |
| activity | n. | 活动 | What **activities** did you do? |

### 🔑 高频短语

| 短语 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| go on vacation | 去度假 | Did you **go on vacation** last summer? |

## 💡 易错点

### 1. nothing / anything 区别

| 情况 | 用法 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 否定句 | **anything** | I didn't do **anything**. ✅ |
| 否定句 | ~~nothing~~ | I didn't do **nothing**. ❌（双重否定） |
| 肯定句 | **nothing** | I did **nothing** special. ✅ |

### 2. few / a few 区别

- **a few** = 有一些（肯定含义）→ I have **a few** friends. （有几个朋友，挺好的）
- **few** = 几乎没有（否定含义）→ I have **few** friends. （朋友很少，有点可怜）

### 3. spend 的搭配

```
spend + 时间/金钱 + on sth.        → spend two hours on homework
spend + 时间/金钱 + (in) doing sth. → spend two hours (in) reading
```
❌ ~~I spend two hours to do homework.~~（不能接不定式）

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（填空）：** Last summer, our volleyball team went to Shaolin Temple. We ________ (spend) two days there and had a wonderful time.

> 📝 **答案**：spent  
> **解析**：last summer 是过去时间标志，spend 变过去式 spent。

**题2（改错）：** I didn't do nothing special during the vacation. Find and correct the mistake.

> 📝 **答案**：nothing → anything  
> **解析**：否定句（didn't）中不能再用 nothing，否则构成双重否定，改为 anything。
