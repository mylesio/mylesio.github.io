## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

你们队里谁跳得最高？谁扣球最猛？谁防守最稳？这些"最..."全都要用**最高级**来表达！Unit 7 的核心语法就是形容词/副词最高级，中考年年必考，今天彻底搞清楚！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 最高级构成规则

#### 形容词最高级变化

| 规则 | 变化方式 | 例词 |
|------|----------|------|
| 一般单音节 | +est | tall→**tallest**, high→**highest**, long→**longest** |
| 以e结尾 | +st | large→**largest**, wide→**widest** |
| 辅音+y结尾 | y→i+est | heavy→**heaviest**, busy→**busiest** |
| 重读闭音节(辅元辅) | 双写末字母+est | big→**biggest**, hot→**hottest** |
| 多音节（两音节以上）| the most + 原形 | beautiful→**the most beautiful** |
| 部分双音节 | 两种均可 | clever→**cleverest/most clever** |
| 不规则变化 | 特殊记忆 | good→**best**, bad→**worst**, many→**most**, far→**farthest** |

---

### 最高级句型结构

```
the + 形容词最高级 + 名词 + 介词短语（范围）

常用范围介词：
in + 地点/团体     She is the tallest in our class.
of + 复数名词      He is the best of all the players.
```

**注意：** 范围是地方或组织 → 用 **in**；范围是一组人/物 → 用 **of**

```
the tallest in China ✅（国家=地点，用 in）
the tallest of the three players ✅（三人中，用 of）
the tallest in the three players ❌（人群不是地点）
```

---

### 原级、比较级、最高级 三级对比

| 级别 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 原级（平级）| as + adj. + as | She is as tall as Li Ming. |
| 比较级 | adj.-er / more adj. + than | She is taller than Li Ming. |
| 最高级 | the + adj.-est / the most adj. | She is the tallest in the team. |

**地理信息对比练习：**

| 地理事实 | 英文表达 |
|----------|----------|
| 珠峰 = 世界最高山 | Mt. Everest is the highest mountain in the world. |
| 亚马逊 = 最长（水流）河 | The Amazon is the longest river by water flow. |
| 太平洋 = 最大海洋 | The Pacific is the largest ocean. |
| 贝加尔湖 = 最深湖 | Lake Baikal is the deepest lake. |
| 撒哈拉 = 最大热带沙漠 | The Sahara is the largest hot desert. |

---

### 副词最高级

副词最高级一般不加 **the**（当句中有 of all / in the team 等明确范围时可加 the）：

```
She jumps the highest of all the players. ✅
She runs fastest on the team. ✅（也可不加 the）
He played best in the final match. ✅
```

---

### 最高级特殊句型

#### "one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词"

```
Mount Everest is one of the highest mountains in the world.
（one of 后面名词要用复数！）
```

❌ one of the highest mountain  
✅ one of the highest **mountains**

#### "Which is + 最高级...?"

```
Which is the highest mountain in the world?
Which city has the largest population in China?
```

---

## 💡 易错点

### 1. 最高级前必须有 the

❌ She is **tallest** player in our class.  
✅ She is **the tallest** player in our class.

例外：当最高级作表语且有物主代词时可省：
> She tried **her best**.（不用 the）

### 2. in vs of 范围选择

```
in + 地点/组织/团体（单数概念）
of + 复数名词/数字

She is the best player in our school. ✅（学校=地点）
She is the best of the five players. ✅（五个人中）
```

### 3. one of + 复数！

❌ one of the best **player**  
✅ one of the best **players**

### 4. most 别忘搭配 the

❌ She is **most** beautiful girl.  
✅ She is **the most** beautiful girl.

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（用括号内词的适当形式）：**
1. The Amazon is one of the ________ (long) rivers in the world.
2. Which subject is ________ (difficult) for you?
3. Li Fang jumped ________ (high) of all the players in the game.

> 📝 **答案**：1. longest　2. the most difficult　3. the highest

**题2（单选）：** China is ________ country in the world by population.

> A. a most large　B. the most large　C. the largest　D. a largest

> 📝 **答案：C**（large 是单音节形容词，最高级加 -est，前面加 the）

**题3（改错）：** Our school has one of the best sport team in the city.

> 📝 **答案**：sport team → **sports teams**（one of 后接复数）
