## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

赢了比赛去看电影庆祝！你会选哪家影院？Unit 4 教你用英语评价"最好、最差、最近、最贵"——形容词**最高级**全在这里！

## 📚 核心内容

| 单词 | 词性 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| theater | n. | 剧院；电影院 | This is the best movie **theater** in town. |
| comfortable | adj. | 舒适的 | The seats are very **comfortable**. |
| fresh | adj. | 新鲜的 | I love **fresh** popcorn! |
| close | adj./adv. | 近的；靠近 | The cinema is **close** to our school. |

| expensive | adj. | 昂贵的 | This restaurant is too **expensive**. |
| large | adj. | 大的 | They have the **largest** screen in the city. |
| screen | n. | 屏幕；银幕 | The **screen** is 20 meters wide! |
| seat | n. | 座位 | Every **seat** in the theater is comfortable. |
| crowd | n. | 人群 | There was a big **crowd** at the game. |
| service | n. | 服务 | The **service** here is excellent. |
| survey | n./v. | 调查 | We did a **survey** about movie theaters. |

### 🔑 最高级相关词汇

| 词汇 | 中文 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| the best | 最好的 | This is **the best** team I've seen. |
| the worst | 最差的 | That was **the worst** game this season. |
| the most | 最多的；最… | She is **the most** talented player. |
| the least | 最少的；最不… | This is **the least** expensive option. |
| the nearest | 最近的 | What is **the nearest** hospital? |
| the biggest | 最大的 | It has **the biggest** screen. |
| the cheapest | 最便宜的 | This cinema is **the cheapest**. |

---

### 🔑 评价性短语

| 短语 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| the best ... in + 地点 | 在…中最好的 | **the best** theater **in** town |
| the most ... of all | 在所有…中最… | **the most** popular **of all** |
| think of | 认为；想到 | What do you **think of** this place? |
| have good service | 服务好 | This restaurant **has good service**. |
| be close to | 离…近 | The theater **is close to** school. |

## 💡 易错点

### 1. close / closely / near 的区别

| 词 | 用法 | 例句 |
|----|------|------|
| **close** (adj.) | 近的（形容词，作表语或定语） | The school is **close** to home. |
| **close** (adv.) | 靠近地（副词，修饰动词） | Come **close** and listen. |
| **closely** (adv.) | 仔细地（强调仔细/紧密） | Watch the game **closely**. |
| **near** (prep./adj.) | 在附近；近的 | He lives **near** our school. |

❌ The cinema is **closely** to school. → ✅ The cinema is **close** to school.

### 2. comfortable 拼写

❌ confortable / confortble  
✅ **com-for-ta-ble** (4音节)

> 🏐 记忆技巧：comfort（舒适）+ able（能够）= comfortable（能够舒适）

### 3. expensive vs cheap 比较级/最高级

| 原级 | 比较级 | 最高级 |
|------|--------|--------|
| expensive | more expensive | the most expensive |
| cheap | cheaper | the cheapest |
| good | better | the best |
| bad | worse | the worst |

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（填空）：** The new shopping mall has the ________ (large) cinema screen in our city. Every seat is ________ (comfortable) than in the old cinema.

> 📝 **答案**：largest；more comfortable  
> **解析**：第一空是最高级（in our city限定范围）；第二空有 than，用比较级。

**题2（翻译）：** 这是我们市里最好的电影院，它离我们学校很近，票价也最便宜。

> 📝 **参考答案**：This is the best movie theater in our city. It is close to our school and has the cheapest tickets.
