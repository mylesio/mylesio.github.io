和队友闹矛盾？学习压力太大？今天学的词汇教你用英语倾诉烦恼、给出建议。排球队里的「心理辅导师」就是你！

## 📚 核心内容

### 重点单词表

| 单词 | 词性 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| problem | n. | 问题；烦恼 | I have a big **problem** with my coach. |
| argue | v. | 争吵；争论 | I always **argue** with my best friend. |
| communication | n. | 沟通；交流 | Good **communication** solves most problems. |
| nervous | adj. | 紧张的 | I feel **nervous** before every big game. |
| pressure | n. | 压力 | There's too much **pressure** on me. |
| compare | v. | 比较 | Don't **compare** me to my sister! |
| allow | v. | 允许 | My parents don't **allow** me to play outside. |
| complain | v. | 抱怨 | Stop **complaining** and do something! |
| refuse | v. | 拒绝 | She **refused** to talk to me. |
| offer | v. | 提供；主动给 | He **offered** to help me study. |
| get on with | phr. | 与……相处 | I don't **get on with** my classmates. |
| in common | phr. | 共同点 | We have a lot **in common**. |
| talk about | phr. | 谈论 | Let's **talk about** this problem. |
| instead of | prep. | 代替；而不是 | Try talking **instead of** arguing. |
| both...and... | conj. | 两者都 | **Both** my parents **and** I need to talk. |
| either...or... | conj. | 要么……要么 | **Either** stay or leave. |
| neither...nor... | conj. | 两者都不 | **Neither** of us is right. |
| hurt | v./adj. | 伤害；受伤的 | Her words really **hurt** me. |
| miss | v. | 想念；错过 | I **miss** my old teammates. |
| relationship | n. | 关系 | Our team has a great **relationship**. |

### 重要短语搭配

| 短语 | 意思 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| argue with sb. | 和某人争吵 | I argued **with** my mom. |
| argue about sth. | 为某事争论 | We argued **about** homework. |
| get on with sb. | 与某人相处 | I **get on with** my teammates well. |
| in common | 有共同点 | We have nothing **in common**. |
| instead of | 代替；而不是 | Talk **instead of** fighting. |
| allow sb. to do | 允许某人做 | My mom allows me **to** stay up late. |

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **allow sb. to do sth.**（允许某人做某事）  
   ✅ My parents **allow me to** play volleyball.  
   ❌ My parents allow me **play** volleyball.

2. **communicate 是动词，communication 是名词**  
   ✅ Good **communication** is important.（名词用法）  
   ✅ We need to **communicate** more.（动词用法）

3. **compare A with/to B**  
   - compare A **with** B = 比较两者异同  
   - compare A **to** B = 把A比作B  
   ✅ Don't compare me **with** my sister.

4. **refuse to do** 拒绝做某事（不是 refuse doing）  
   ✅ She refused **to talk** to me.  ❌ She refused **talking**.

5. **hurt** 过去式也是 **hurt**（不规则动词，三态同形）  
   hurt – hurt – hurt
