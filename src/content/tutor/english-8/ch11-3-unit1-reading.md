## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

中考阅读里经常出现"给出建议"类文章。今天这篇短文和你的排球训练超级相关——学会读，也学会说！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

> **Keeping Healthy as a Student Athlete**
>
> Being a student athlete is exciting, but it also brings many challenges. Many young players often feel tired after long training sessions. Here are some common health problems and what you should do about them.
>
> **Sore muscles and ankle pain** are very common in volleyball. If you have sore muscles, you should rest and put ice on the area for 15 minutes. You shouldn't train again until the pain goes away. If your ankle is swollen, you should see a doctor right away.
>
> **Headaches** often happen when players don't drink enough water. You should drink at least 8 glasses of water every day. During a game, drink water every 20 minutes.
>
> **Stomachaches** before a big game are sometimes caused by nervousness. You should eat light food two hours before the game. You shouldn't eat too much or drink cold water right before you play.
>
> Remember: your health is more important than any game. If you feel really sick, you should tell your coach and take a rest. A healthy athlete plays better!

---

### 词汇注释

| 单词/短语 | 意思 |
|-----------|------|
| challenge | 挑战 |
| sore muscles | 肌肉酸痛 |
| swollen | 肿胀的 |
| at least | 至少 |
| nervousness | 紧张感 |
| light food | 清淡食物 |
| coach | 教练 |

---

### 阅读理解题

**1. 根据文章，排球运动员最常见的伤是什么？**

**2. 头痛的主要原因是什么？应该怎么做？**

**3. 赛前肚子痛的原因是什么？**

**4. 文章最后一段想告诉我们什么？**

---

### 参考答案

1. Sore muscles and ankle pain.（肌肉酸痛和脚踝疼痛）

2. 原因：喝水不够。应对：每天至少喝8杯水，比赛中每20分钟喝一次。

3. 赛前紧张（nervousness）。

4. 健康比比赛更重要；感觉不舒服要告诉教练休息。

---

### 句子仿写（写作练习）

参考短文，用 should/shouldn't 写出你的运动建议，不少于3句：

```
例：If you feel tired after training, you should rest and sleep early.
    You shouldn't train with a fever.
    You should drink enough water every day.
```

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **right away = immediately** 马上，表紧迫，中考常见替换词  

2. **go away** 在这里是"消失"的意思，不是"走开"  
   The pain should go away after rest.（休息后疼痛应该消失）

3. **at least** 至少，反义词是 **at most**（最多）

4. **be caused by** 被……引起，被动结构：  
   Stomachaches **are caused by** nervousness.
