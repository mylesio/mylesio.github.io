## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

明天有排球训练赛，队长说："明天我**要去**打比赛，你们呢？"  
这种"已经计划好要做的事"，英语用 **be going to** 来表达。  
今天彻底搞懂它——中考每年必考！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 一、be going to 的基本结构

```
主语 + am/is/are + going to + 动词原形
```

| 主语 | be动词 | going to | 动词原形 |
|------|--------|----------|----------|
| I | am | going to | study |
| He/She/It | is | going to | be |
| We/You/They | are | going to | play |

**正面例句：**
- I **am going to** study computer science. 我打算学计算机科学。
- She **is going to** be a doctor. 她打算当医生。
- They **are going to** play volleyball tomorrow. 他们明天打算打排球。

---

### 二、否定句

```
主语 + am/is/are + NOT + going to + 动词原形
```

- I **am not going to** watch TV tonight. 我今晚不打算看电视。
- He **isn't going to** come to the party. 他不打算来聚会。

---

### 三、疑问句与回答

**一般疑问句：**
```
Am/Is/Are + 主语 + going to + 动词原形?
```

- **Are** you going to study tonight?  
  → Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.

- **Is** she going to be a pilot?  
  → Yes, she is. / No, she isn't.

**特殊疑问句：**
```
疑问词 + am/is/are + 主语 + going to + 动词原形?
```

- **What** are you going to be when you grow up?  
  → I'm going to be an engineer.
  
- **When** is he going to leave?  
  → He's going to leave at 8 o'clock.

---

### 四、be going to vs. will 的区别（重点！）

| | be going to | will |
|--|-------------|------|
| **用法** | 事先计划好的事 | 临时决定/预测/承诺 |
| **例句** | I'm going to study tonight.（昨晚就计划好了） | I'll help you with that.（刚才临时决定帮你） |
| **信号词** | tomorrow, next week, this weekend | probably, I think, maybe |

**对比例句：**
- **I'm going to buy** a new bike this weekend.（早就计划好了）
- **I'll buy** you a coffee! （现在临时决定请你喝咖啡）

> 🏐 **排球记忆法：**  
> be going to = 赛前战术计划（教练早想好了）  
> will = 比赛中临场决策（突然改变战术）

---

### 五、常见时间状语（与be going to搭配）

| 时间状语 | 含义 |
|----------|------|
| tomorrow | 明天 |
| next week/month/year | 下周/月/年 |
| this weekend | 这个周末 |
| in the future | 将来 |
| when I grow up | 当我长大 |
| this evening / tonight | 今晚 |

---

## 💡 易错点

### ❌ 错误1：be动词与主语不一致
- ✅ She **is** going to study hard.
- ❌ She **am** going to study hard.

### ❌ 错误2：going to 后接动词原形，不加 to be 重复
- ✅ I am going to **be** a doctor.
- ❌ I am going to **being** a doctor.

### ❌ 错误3：否定句位置错误
- ✅ I **am not** going to watch TV.
- ❌ I am going **not** to watch TV.

### ❌ 错误4：疑问句忘记提前be动词
- ✅ **Is** she going to join us?
- ❌ She **is** going to join us?（这是陈述句语调疑问，书面语中不规范）

### ❌ 错误5：混淆 be going to 和 will
- 看到"已经计划好" → be going to
- 看到"临时决定" / "认为将会" → will

---

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题目1（句型转换）：**  
She is going to visit her grandparents next Sunday.（改为否定句）  
→ **She is not going to visit her grandparents next Sunday.**

**题目2（翻译）：**  
你长大后打算做什么？  
→ **What are you going to do when you grow up?**  
或：**What are you going to be when you grow up?**

**题目3（选择）：**  
— I don't know what to do with all this homework!  
— Don't worry. I ______ help you.  
A. am going to　　B. will　　C. was going to　　D. am  
→ **答案：B（will）**  
解析：这是听到对方说话后"临时决定"帮助，用 will，不用 be going to。
