## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

训练结束回家，妈妈说："Could you please clean your room?" 这句话你听懂了吗？今天学家务词汇，让你在家里也能"全场布防"！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 重点单词表

| 单词 | 词性 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| clean | v./adj. | 打扫；干净的 | Can you **clean** your room today? |
| dirty | adj. | 脏的 | My volleyball jersey is really **dirty**. |
| wash | v. | 洗 | I need to **wash** the dishes after dinner. |
| fold | v. | 折叠 | Please **fold** the clothes neatly. |
| sweep | v. | 扫（地）| Could you **sweep** the floor? |
| rubbish | n. | 垃圾（英式） | Take out the **rubbish** before you go. |
| trash | n. | 垃圾（美式） | Don't forget to take out the **trash**. |
| borrow | v. | 借（入）| Can I **borrow** your umbrella? |
| lend | v. | 借（出）| I'll **lend** you my jacket. |
| do the dishes | phr. | 洗碗 | It's your turn to **do the dishes**. |
| make the bed | phr. | 铺床 | I **make the bed** every morning. |
| do the laundry | phr. | 洗衣服 | Mom **does the laundry** on weekends. |
| take out | phr.v. | 拿出；取出 | **Take out** the trash, please. |
| mess | n. | 乱七八糟 | Your room is a total **mess**! |
| tidy | v./adj. | 整理；整洁的 | **Tidy** up your desk before studying. |
| floor | n. | 地板 | Sweep the **floor** after dinner. |
| chore | n. | 家务杂务 | We share **chores** in our family. |
| enough | adj./adv. | 足够的 | I've done **enough** chores today! |
| fair | adj. | 公平的 | It's not **fair** that I do all the chores. |
| however | adv. | 然而；但是 | I'm tired; **however**, I'll help. |

---

### 家务词汇分类

**厨房类：**
- do the dishes（洗碗）
- cook dinner（做晚饭）
- take out the trash/rubbish（倒垃圾）

**卧室类：**
- make the bed（铺床）
- fold the clothes（叠衣服）
- tidy up the room（整理房间）

**客厅/地面类：**
- sweep the floor（扫地）
- mop the floor（拖地）
- vacuum the carpet（吸地毯）

**洗涤类：**
- do the laundry（洗衣服）
- wash the car（洗车）
- clean the windows（擦窗户）

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **borrow vs. lend 方向相反**  
   - borrow = 借入（我从别人那借）→ borrow sth. from sb.  
   - lend = 借出（我把东西借给别人）→ lend sth. to sb. / lend sb. sth.  
   ✅ Can I **borrow** your pen?（我借你的笔）  
   ✅ I'll **lend** you my pen.（我把笔借给你）

2. **trash / rubbish 都是"垃圾"**，美式用 trash，英式用 rubbish，中考两个都考  

3. **do the dishes** 洗碗，注意 dishes 用复数  

4. **fair** 在这里是"公平"，不是"集市"或"金发"  

5. **chore** 复数 chores，家务杂活；注意拼写 ch-o-r-e-s
