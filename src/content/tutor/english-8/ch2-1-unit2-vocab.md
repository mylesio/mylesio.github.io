## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

教练问："How often do you exercise?" 你会说"每天！"还是"偶尔……"？Unit 2 的词汇全都跟健康生活和频率有关——这对排球运动员来说太重要了！

### 🔑 频率词汇（Unit 2 核心！）

| 频率词 | 频率 | 例句 |
|--------|------|------|
| always | 100% | I **always** stretch before practice. |
| usually | 80% | She **usually** goes to bed at 10. |
| often | 60% | We **often** play matches on weekends. |
| sometimes | 40% | I **sometimes** feel nervous before games. |
| hardly ever | 10% | He **hardly ever** eats junk food. |
| never | 0% | I **never** skip morning training. |

---

### 🔑 高频短语

| 短语 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| how often | 多久一次 | **How often** do you exercise? |
| at least | 至少 | Exercise at **least** three times a week. |
| once a week | 每周一次 | I swim **once a week**. |
| twice a month | 每月两次 | We have a match **twice a month**. |
| be good for | 对…有益 | Sport **is good for** your health. |
| try to do | 尽力做 | I **try to** eat healthy food. |

---

## 💡 易错点

### 1. 频率副词的位置

```
频率副词 + 实义动词（位于动词前）
She usually exercises in the morning. ✅
She exercises usually in the morning. ❌

频率副词 + be动词后
He is always late for training. ✅
He always is late for training. ❌
```

### 2. once / twice / three times

- **once** = 一次（特殊形式，不说 one time）
- **twice** = 两次（特殊形式，不说 two times）
- **three/four... times** = 三次/四次...

❌ I exercise two times a week. → ✅ I exercise **twice** a week.

### 3. how often vs how long vs how soon

| 疑问词 | 含义 | 答语 |
|--------|------|------|
| **how often** | 多久一次（频率） | once a day, never... |
| **how long** | 多长时间（时长） | for two hours... |
| **how soon** | 多快（距将来的时间） | in ten minutes... |

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（填空）：** ________ do you practice volleyball? — About three times a week.

> 📝 **答案**：How often  
> **解析**：回答是"三次"（频率），疑问词用 How often。

**题2（改错）：** She is such healthy girl that she never gets sick.

> 📝 **答案**：such healthy girl → such **a** healthy girl  
> **解析**：such + a/an + 形容词 + 可数单数名词；healthy girl 是可数名词单数，需要加冠词 a。
