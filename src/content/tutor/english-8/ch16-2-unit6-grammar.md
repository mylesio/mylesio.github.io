## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

昨天训练时教练说了什么？你们打了几局？——讲**过去发生**的事，就要用**一般过去时**！Unit 6 的愚公移山全程用过去时叙事，掌握它，中考叙述类作文妥了！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 一般过去时 核心规则

#### 基本结构

| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + 动词过去式 | The old man **moved** rocks every day. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + did not/didn't + 动词原形 | He **didn't give up**. |
| 疑问句 | Did + 主语 + 动词原形？ | **Did** the gods **help** him? |
| 特殊疑问 | Wh- + did + 主语 + 动词原形？ | **Why did** he want to move the mountains? |

---

### 规则动词变化规则

| 规则 | 变化方式 | 例词 |
|------|----------|------|
| 一般词尾 | +ed | work→worked, play→played |
| 以e结尾 | +d | move→moved, live→lived |
| 辅音+y结尾 | y→i，+ed | try→**tried**, carry→**carried** |
| 重读闭音节（辅元辅）| 双写末辅音+ed | stop→**stopped**, plan→**planned** |

---

### 不规则动词（Unit 6 重点）

| 原形 | 过去式 | 中文 |
|------|--------|------|
| begin | began | 开始 |
| tell | told | 告诉 |
| give | gave | 给 |
| think | thought | 认为 |
| feel | felt | 感觉 |
| come | came | 来 |
| say | said | 说 |
| hear | heard | 听到 |
| see | saw | 看见 |
| know | knew | 知道 |

---

### 时间状语（过去时的标志词）

| 时间状语 | 中文 | 例句 |
|----------|------|------|
| yesterday | 昨天 | I trained **yesterday**. |
| last week/year | 上周/去年 | **Last year**, we won the championship. |
| ...ago | …前 | The story began **thousands of years ago**. |
| in + 年份 | 在…年 | She joined the team **in 2020**. |
| just now | 刚才 | The coach called me **just now**. |
| once upon a time | 从前 | **Once upon a time**, there was an old man. |

---

### 一般过去时 vs 一般现在时

| 对比维度 | 一般现在时 | 一般过去时 |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| 表示内容 | 现在的状态/习惯 | 过去发生的事 |
| 时间状语 | every day, always, usually | yesterday, last year, ago |
| 动词形式 | 原形/第三人称单数+s | 过去式（-ed 或不规则） |
| 否定 | don't/doesn't | didn't |
| 疑问 | Do/Does | Did |

**例句对比：**
```
现在：She plays volleyball every afternoon.（现在习惯）
过去：She played volleyball yesterday afternoon.（昨天发生）

现在：The team doesn't give up easily.（现在特点）
过去：The team didn't give up in the finals.（决赛时）
```

---

### 故事叙述技巧

用过去时讲愚公故事的框架：

```
Long ago / Once upon a time...（开头）
...tried/wanted/decided to...（中间事件）
...finally/in the end...（结局）
```

**示例：**
> Long ago, there was an old man called Yugong. He lived near two big mountains. Every day, he **tried** to move the mountains. His neighbors **laughed at** him, but he **didn't give up**. Finally, the gods **were moved by** his spirit and **took away** the mountains.

---

## 💡 易错点

### 1. 否定和疑问句用原形！

❌ He **didn't moved** the mountain.  
✅ He **didn't move** the mountain.

❌ **Did** he **tried** hard?  
✅ **Did** he **try** hard?

> **规律**：有 did/didn't 时，动词回到原形！

### 2. try→tried（不是 tryed）

辅音+y 结尾 → y 变 i + ed

```
try → tried ✅   (not tryed ❌)
carry → carried ✅
study → studied ✅
```

### 3. 时间状语位置

时间状语可放句尾（常用）或句首（强调）：
```
She joined the volleyball team last year. ✅（句尾）
Last year, she joined the volleyball team. ✅（句首，加逗号）
She last year joined the volleyball team. ❌（不能夹在中间）
```

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（用动词适当形式填空）：**  
The old man ______ (begin) to move the mountains a long time ago. His son and grandson ______ (continue) his work after him.

> 📝 **答案**：began；continued

**题2（单句改错）：**  
Did the old man gave up when people laughed at him?

> 📝 **答案**：gave → **give**（did 后面动词用原形）

**题3（翻译）：**  
从前，有个老人住在两座大山旁边。他决定移走这两座山。

> 📝 **参考答案**：Once upon a time, there was an old man who lived near two big mountains. He decided to move the mountains.
