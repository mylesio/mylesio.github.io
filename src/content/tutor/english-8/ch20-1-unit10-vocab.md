## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

"你打排球多久了？" "我从小学三年级开始打，到现在三年了！" —— 这句话英文怎么说？Unit 10 的核心是 **for**（持续多久）和 **since**（从什么时候开始）！今天先学词汇，后天语法课你会豁然开朗！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 重点单词表

| 单词 | 词性 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| for | prep. | 持续了…（一段时间） | I have played volleyball **for** three years. |
| since | prep./conj. | 自从…以来 | She has trained here **since** 2022. |
| own | v./adj. | 拥有；自己的 | Do you **own** a bicycle? |
| keep | v. | 保持；保留；继续 | She **keeps** practicing every day. |
| still | adv. | 仍然；还 | She **still** uses the same volleyball shoes. |
| over | prep. | 超过；经过 | **Over** the past year, she has improved a lot. |
| collect | v. | 收集 | She **collects** jerseys from every team she plays. |
| item | n. | 物品；项目；条款 | This jersey is her most treasured **item**. |
| special | adj. | 特别的；专门的 | This day is very **special** to her. |
| memory | n. | 记忆；回忆 | She has many happy **memories** of volleyball. |
| childhood | n. | 童年；少年时代 | She started playing volleyball in her **childhood**. |
| moment | n. | 时刻；片刻 | Every game creates a new **moment** to remember. |
| interest | n./v. | 兴趣；使感兴趣 | Her **interest** in volleyball started early. |
| prize | n. | 奖品；奖励 | She won a **prize** at the competition. |
| medal | n. | 奖牌；勋章 | She earned three **medals** last season. |
| jersey | n. | 球衣；运动衫 | She wore her team **jersey** with pride. |
| since | conj. | 自从（引导时间状语从句）| She has improved a lot **since** she joined the team. |
| recently | adv. | 最近；不久前 | Have you practiced any new skills **recently**? |
| pass | v. | 经过；传球；通过 | Three years have **passed** since she started playing. |
| member | n. | 成员；会员 | She has been a **member** of the team for two years. |

---

### 🔑 for vs since（词汇预热）

| 用 for | 用 since |
|--------|---------|
| for a week（一周）| since last week（自从上周）|
| for three years（三年）| since 2022（自从2022年）|
| for a long time（很久）| since I was 10（自从我10岁）|
| for two months（两个月）| since Monday（自从周一）|

> **规律预习**：for 后面接**一段时间**，since 后面接**时间点**！

---

### 🔑 时间段 vs 时间点

| 时间段（用 for） | 时间点（用 since）|
|---------|---------|
| two years（两年）| 2022 |
| six months（六个月）| last year（去年）|
| a long time（很久）| yesterday（昨天）|
| three weeks（三周）| I was born（我出生时）|
| ages（好久）| she joined the team（她加入球队时）|

## 💡 易错点

### 1. own 的两种词性

```
own = adj. 自己的（放在 my/his/her 后加强语气）
She has her own volleyball. = 她有自己的排球（是自己的，不是借的）

own = v. 拥有
Do you own a bike? = 你有自行车吗？
She owns this jersey. = 这件球衣是她的。
```

### 2. keep + doing（继续做某事）

```
keep + doing = 继续做/坚持做
She keeps practicing even on weekends. ✅
keep 后面接动名词，不接不定式
❌ She keeps to practice.
✅ She keeps practicing.
```

### 3. memory 可数/不可数

```
a memory = 一个回忆（可数）
memories = 回忆们（复数）
memory = 记忆力（不可数）

She has beautiful memories of the championship. ✅（回忆，可数）
She has a good memory. ✅（记忆力好，不可数）
```

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（单选）：** — How long have you been a member of the volleyball team?  
— ________ I was in Grade Six.

> A. For　B. Since　C. In　D. From

> 📝 **答案：B**（since 后接时间点：since I was in Grade Six）

**题2（翻译）：** 她从2020年开始就一直是这支球队的队长了。

> 📝 **参考答案**：She has been the captain of this team since 2020.
