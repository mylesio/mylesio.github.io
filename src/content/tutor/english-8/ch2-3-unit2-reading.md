## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

学校对八年级同学做了一次"健康生活方式"调查。看看你的生活习惯跟他们比怎么样？排球运动员的生活应该是最健康的！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

> **A Healthy Life Survey**
>
> We did a survey of 100 students at Zhongzhou Middle School about their habits. Here are the results.
>
> About **exercise**: 30% of students exercise every day. 45% exercise two or three times a week. The rest — about 25% — hardly ever exercise. Most students say they are too busy with their homework to find time for sports.
>
> About **eating habits**: Only 20% of students say they always eat vegetables and fruit. 50% eat fast food sometimes. Shockingly, 15% eat junk food almost every day! The school nurse says this is very unhealthy.
>
> About **screen time**: 60% of students spend more than two hours on their phones after school. Only 10% spend less than 30 minutes. The most popular activities are watching short videos and playing online games.
>
> About **sleep**: Most students (70%) go to bed before 11 pm. However, 30% stay up past midnight on weekends.
>
> The survey shows that while some students have good habits, many still need to improve. As our PE teacher often says: "Your body is your most important teammate. Take care of it!"

---

### 阅读理解

**1. What percentage of students exercise every day?**

A. 25%　　B. 30%　　C. 45%　　D. 70%

> 📝 答案：**B**（原文"30% of students exercise every day"）

**2. What is the MOST popular screen activity among students?**

A. Doing homework　　B. Reading e-books  
C. Watching short videos and playing games　　D. Exercising online

> 📝 答案：**C**（原文"The most popular activities are watching short videos and playing online games"）

**3. According to the passage, why don't many students exercise?**

A. They don't like sports.  
B. They are too busy with their homework.  
C. They prefer watching TV.  
D. Their school doesn't have a gym.

> 📝 答案：**B**（原文"they are too busy with their homework to find time for sports"）

**4. What does the PE teacher mean by "Your body is your most important teammate"?**

A. You should play sports with your teammates.  
B. You should take good care of your health.  
C. A good body makes you popular.  
D. Teammates are more important than teachers.

> 📝 答案：**B**（推理题：最重要的队友=必须照顾好，暗指要保持健康）

---

### 综合运用：根据短文完成句子

1. The survey found that ________ percent of students **hardly ever** exercise.
2. The school nurse thinks that eating junk food almost every day is very ________.
3. Most students spend more than two hours on their ________ after school.

> 📝 答案：
> 1. 25（原文"25% — hardly ever exercise"）
> 2. unhealthy（原文"very unhealthy"）
> 3. phones（原文"on their phones after school"）

---

## 💡 易错点

### 阅读中的数字和百分比

- 做数字题时，**每个选项对应原文不同数字**，一定要逐一核对
- 注意 "the rest" = 剩下的那些（要用 100% 减去前面所有的）
- "shockingly" 这类副词是作者情感态度的标志词，可帮助判断作者观点

### 推理题技巧

> 🏐 **排球类比**：推理题就像在球场上预判对手——不是看他现在做什么，是根据他的动作预判下一步。读文章也一样：不找直接答案，看整体逻辑。

**写作练习（中考常考）：** 请用3-4句话描述你的健康习惯，包括运动频率、饮食和睡眠。

> 📝 **参考答案**：  
> I have good habits to stay healthy. I exercise every day because I am on the school volleyball team. I usually eat vegetables and fruit, and I hardly ever eat junk food. I always go to bed before 10:30 pm to get enough sleep.
