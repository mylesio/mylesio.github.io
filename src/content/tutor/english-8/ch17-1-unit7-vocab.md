## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

你知道世界最高的排球网有多高？——好吧，那个不重要。但你知道世界最高的山是哪座吗？Unit 7 学地理+自然知识，同时复习**最高级**（the tallest, the longest...）！今天先拿下词汇！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 重点单词表

| 单词 | 词性 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| deep | adj. | 深的 | The Pacific Ocean is very **deep**. |
| long | adj. | 长的 | The Nile is the **longest** river in the world. |
| high | adj. | 高的（海拔） | How **high** is Mount Everest? |
| tall | adj. | 高的（人/建筑物） | She is the **tallest** player on our team. |
| large | adj. | 大的（面积） | China is a very **large** country. |
| wide | adj. | 宽的 | The Yellow River is very **wide**. |
| population | n. | 人口 | China has the largest **population** in the world. |
| area | n. | 面积；地区 | Russia has the largest **area** of any country. |
| river | n. | 河流 | The Amazon is the largest **river** by water flow. |
| ocean | n. | 海洋 | The Pacific is the largest **ocean**. |
| desert | n. | 沙漠 | The Sahara is the largest hot **desert**. |
| country | n. | 国家；农村 | How many **countries** are there in the world? |
| continent | n. | 洲；大陆 | Asia is the largest **continent**. |
| measure | v. | 测量；衡量 | Scientists **measured** the height of Everest. |
| kilometer | n. | 千米；公里 | The mountain is 8,849 **kilometers**... wait, meters! |
| meter | n. | 米（长度单位） | The net is 2.43 **meters** high for men. |
| far | adj./adv. | 远的；远地 | How **far** is it from here? |
| nearly | adv. | 几乎；将近 | It's **nearly** 9,000 meters high. |
| exactly | adv. | 确切地；正是 | **Exactly** how tall is she? |
| active | adj. | 活跃的；活动的 | An **active** volcano can erupt at any time. |

### 🔑 形容词 vs 最高级（Unit 7 核心）

| 原级 | 比较级 | 最高级 | 中文 |
|------|--------|--------|------|
| high | higher | the highest | 高的（海拔/分数/程度）|
| tall | taller | the tallest | 高的（人/建筑）|
| long | longer | the longest | 长的 |
| deep | deeper | the deepest | 深的 |
| large | larger | the largest | 大的（面积）|
| wide | wider | the widest | 宽的 |
| far | farther/further | the farthest/furthest | 远的 |
| many/much | more | the most | 多的 |

| 山脉 | mountain, peak, hill |

### 🔑 high vs tall 区分

| 用 high | 用 tall |
|---------|---------|
| 山的海拔高度 | 人的身高 |
| 建筑物的楼层高度（抽象） | 建筑物的实体高度（可见）|
| 飞机的飞行高度 | 树木、灯杆 |
| 得分、分数 | — |

```
Mount Everest is the highest mountain. ✅（山用 high）
She is the tallest player. ✅（人用 tall）
The building is very tall/high. ✅（建筑两者均可）
```

## 💡 易错点

### 1. population 的修饰

❌ China has a **big/large** population.（非正式，但能用）  
✅ China has a **large** population.（推荐，大=large，不用 big）  
✅ China has a **population of** 1.4 billion.（加 of 表数量）

❌ China's population is **more** than India's.（人口多/少不用 more）  
✅ China's population is **larger** than India's.

### 2. far 的比较级

- farther — 表示实际距离
- further — 表示更进一步（抽象或距离均可）
- 中考一般两者都接受，but **further** 更常见

### 3. area vs size

- area：强调面积（可量化）Russia has the largest **area**.
- size：强调大小（笼统）What **size** is this country?

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题1（单选）：** Mount Qomolangma is ________ mountain in the world.

> A. high　B. higher　C. the highest　D. highest

> 📝 **答案：C**（最高级前必须有 the）

**题2（翻译）：** 中国人口是世界上最多的。

> 📝 **参考答案**：China has the largest population in the world.
