## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

排球集训结束！同学小明把暑假经历写成了日记。让我们读一读他的故事，看看你能答对几道题！读完还要挑战综合运用题，展示你的真实水平！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

> **My Summer Vacation**
>
> Last summer, I went to Xi'an with my family. We spent four days there. It was my first time to visit such an amazing city.
>
> On the first day, we visited the Terracotta Warriors. There were thousands of clay soldiers standing in rows. I took many photos and bought a small souvenir for my volleyball coach. She always says that history is important.
>
> On the second day, we tried the local food — the famous "roujiamo" (Chinese hamburger) and cold noodles. They were so delicious that I ate three roujiamos! My mom said I spent too much money on food. But I think it was worth it.
>
> The last two days, it rained quite heavily, so we stayed in the hotel and played card games together. My little sister won every time, which made me a little upset. But we still had a great time.
>
> When I came back, I felt tired but happy. I decided to keep a diary about my trip. I think vacations are not just about relaxing — they help us learn about our country and spend time with our family.

---

### 阅读理解题

**1. How long did the writer stay in Xi'an?**

A. Two days　　B. Three days　　C. Four days　　D. Five days

> 📝 答案：**C**（文中"We spent four days there."）

**2. What did the writer buy for his volleyball coach?**

A. Food　　B. A photo　　C. A card game　　D. A small souvenir

> 📝 答案：**D**（"bought a small souvenir for my volleyball coach"）

**3. Why did they stay in the hotel on the last two days?**

A. They were tired.　　B. It rained heavily.　　C. They didn't like Xi'an.　　D. The hotel was amazing.

> 📝 答案：**B**（"it rained quite heavily, so we stayed in the hotel"）

**4. Which of the following is TRUE according to the passage?**

A. The writer visited Xi'an alone.  
B. The writer's sister lost all the card games.  
C. The writer thinks vacations are only for relaxing.  
D. The writer ate a lot of local food on the second day.

> 📝 答案：**D**（文中吃了三个肉夹馍）  
> A错：他和家人一起去；B错：sister赢了每次；C错：文中说不只是休息

---

### 综合运用：完形填空

Read and fill in the blanks with the correct form of the words.

> Last summer, our volleyball team (1)________ (go) to Zhengzhou for a training camp. We (2)________ (stay) there for five days. Every morning, we (3)________ (practice) for three hours. The coach (4)________ (be) very strict but kind. In the evenings, we (5)________ (visit) some famous places like the Yellow River and Henan Museum. It was a trip that I will never forget.

> 📝 **答案**：  
> (1) went　(2) stayed　(3) practiced　(4) was　(5) visited

---

## 💡 易错点

### 阅读推理技巧

- **细节题**：定位原文关键词，不要凭印象
- **推理题**：答案是文章暗示的，不是直接说的
- **判断正误**：每个选项对照原文逐一排查

> 🏐 **排球思维**：读阅读题就像接发球——眼睛快速定位落点（关键词），身体移动到位（找句子），稳稳接住（答题）！
