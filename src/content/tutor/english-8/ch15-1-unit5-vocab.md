## 📚 核心内容

### 重点单词表

| 单词 | 词性 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|------|
| rainstorm | n. | 暴风雨；暴雨 | The **rainstorm** hit the city last night. |
| thunder | n. | 雷声 | We heard **thunder** in the distance. |
| lightning | n. | 闪电 | **Lightning** flashed across the sky. |
| wind | n. | 风 | The **wind** was blowing hard. |
| blow | v. | 吹（过去式 blew）| The wind **blew** down the tree. |

### 天气词汇分类

**自然灾害类：**
```
rainstorm 暴雨 → thunder 雷 + lightning 闪电 + heavy rain 大雨
earthquake 地震
flood 洪水
hurricane 飓风（台风 typhoon）
drought 干旱
```

**天气描述词：**
```
sunny 晴天    cloudy 阴天    windy 大风天    foggy 有雾
rainy 下雨    snowy 下雪    stormy 暴风雨天
```

**副词：**
```
heavily（大量地）→ rain/snow heavily
suddenly（突然）→ suddenly started
loudly（响亮地）→ thunder loudly
```

## 💡 易错点

1. **thunder vs. lightning**  
   - thunder = 雷（声音）  
   - lightning = 闪电（光）  
   "先看 lightning，再听 thunder"（光比声快！）

2. **injure vs. hurt vs. wound**  
   - injure = 受伤（事故/灾难中）  
   - hurt = 疼；伤害（更日常）  
   - wound = 受伤（战争/武器）

3. **heavily 是副词**，修饰动词  
   ✅ It rained **heavily**.  ❌ It rained **heavy**.

4. **frighten vs. scared**  
   - frighten 是动词：The thunder **frightened** me.  
   - scared 是形容词：I was **scared**.  
   ✅ The storm **frightened** me. = I was **scared** of the storm.

5. **blow 的过去式是 blew**（不是 blowed！）  
   The wind **blew** hard last night. ✅
