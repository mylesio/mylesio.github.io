## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

排球场上，不同性格的队友配合默契才能赢！今天我们读一篇关于"最佳搭档"的文章，不仅练阅读，还练比较级综合运用！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

> **The Perfect Partners**
>
> Many people think that best friends must be very similar to each other. However, research shows that opposites often make great partners — especially on a sports team.
>
> Take Wang Fang and Li Mei, two players on our school volleyball team. Wang Fang is more outgoing than Li Mei. She loves talking to everyone, making jokes, and keeping the team's spirits high. Li Mei, on the other hand, is quieter and more serious. She spends more time studying tactics than anyone else on the team.
>
> Although they are very different in personality, both girls are equally hard-working. Wang Fang trains at least two hours more than the average player each week. Li Mei is even more disciplined — she never misses a single practice session.
>
> Their skills are also different but complementary. Wang Fang is a better spiker — she is taller and can jump higher. Li Mei is a more skilled setter — she is calmer under pressure and makes smarter decisions.
>
> Their coach says: "A good team needs both types of players. Together, Wang Fang and Li Mei are stronger than either of them alone. That's the real power of teamwork."

---

### 阅读理解

**1. What does the writer think makes great partners?**

A. Being very similar to each other.  
B. Having opposite personalities.  
C. Training for the same amount of time.  
D. Being on the same sports team.

> 📝 答案：**B**（"research shows that opposites often make great partners"）

**2. Which of the following is TRUE about Li Mei?**

A. She is more outgoing than Wang Fang.  
B. She never studies tactics.  
C. She is a better spiker than Wang Fang.  
D. She is calmer under pressure than Wang Fang.

> 📝 答案：**D**（原文"Li Mei is a more skilled setter — she is calmer under pressure"）

**3. How much more does Wang Fang train than the average player?**

A. One hour more per week.  
B. Two hours more per week.  
C. Three hours more per week.  
D. The article doesn't say.

> 📝 答案：**B**（"at least two hours more than the average player each week"）

**4. The coach's words suggest that ________.**

A. Wang Fang is a better player than Li Mei.  
B. Individual skills are more important than teamwork.  
C. Different players' strengths can combine for better results.  
D. Only hard-working players can make a good team.

> 📝 答案：**C**（"Together, Wang Fang and Li Mei are stronger than either of them alone."）

---

### 综合运用：比较级填空

根据短文，用比较级完成下面的句子：

1. Wang Fang is ________ (outgoing) than Li Mei.
2. Li Mei is ________ (calm) under pressure than Wang Fang.
3. Wang Fang can jump ________ (high) than most players because she is ________ (tall).
4. Together, they are ________ (strong) than either of them alone.

> 📝 **答案**：
> 1. more outgoing　2. calmer　3. higher; taller　4. stronger

---

## 💡 易错点

### 主题句识别

每段的第一句或最后一句通常是**主题句**，快速定位后再读细节。

> 🏐 **排球打法类比**：读文章第一段就像观察对手发球——先判断大方向（主题），然后才接具体的球（细节题）。

### 比较级在阅读中的信号词

遇到以下词，说明有比较关系：
- **than**（直接比较）
- **however / on the other hand / but**（转折比较）
- **both ... equally**（相同程度）
- **more ... than**（程度比较）

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**翻译题：** 虽然她们性格截然不同，但她们在球场上是最佳搭档。合作让她们比各自单打独斗更强。

> 📝 **参考答案**：Although their personalities are very different, they are the best partners on the court. Working together makes them stronger than either of them alone.
