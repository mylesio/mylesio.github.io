## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

队里同学们对看什么电视节目吵起来了！有人喜欢纪录片，有人爱看肥皂剧，有人觉得游戏节目最好。读完这篇讨论，你支持谁的观点？

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

> **What Should We Watch?**
>
> Our school magazine asked students: "What kind of TV program do you prefer?" Here are three students' opinions.
>
> **Chen Wei (Age 14):** I think game shows are the best! They are exciting and everyone can join in. Last week, I watched a game show about sports knowledge and I learned so much. The host asked questions about volleyball, and I got all the answers right! I would like to see more educational game shows on TV. They help us learn while we have fun.
>
> **Zhang Ling (Age 13):** I prefer documentaries to any other programs. I find them the most interesting and educational. Last month, I watched a documentary about the Chinese women's volleyball team. It showed how hard they trained and how they never gave up. After watching it, I decided to work harder at my volleyball practice. I hope that more young people will watch documentaries instead of wasting time on meaningless shows.
>
> **Liu Yang (Age 15):** Honestly, I don't mind watching different programs as long as they are not too violent or boring. Sometimes I watch sitcoms with my family — we all laugh together. Other times I watch sports programs or the news. I think the most important thing is to watch TV in moderation. Too much screen time is bad for everyone, including volleyball players like me!
>
> All three students agree on one thing: the content of TV programs matters. Whether you want to learn, laugh, or relax, choose programs that are good for your mind and body.

---

### 阅读理解

**1. Why does Chen Wei like game shows?**

A. Because they are always about sports.  
B. Because they are both exciting and educational.  
C. Because the host is very funny.  
D. Because they are the cheapest programs.

> 📝 答案：**B**（"exciting" + "learned so much" → 娱乐+教育）

**2. What did Zhang Ling decide to do after watching the volleyball documentary?**

A. Become a TV host.　　B. Stop watching TV.  
C. Work harder at volleyball practice.　　D. Join the women's volleyball team.

> 📝 答案：**C**（"I decided to work harder at my volleyball practice"）

**3. What does "watch TV in moderation" mean?**

A. Watch TV only in the morning.　　B. Watch TV programs about sports.  
C. Not watch too much TV.　　D. Watch TV with your family.

> 📝 答案：**C**（in moderation = 适量，不过量）

**4. What do all three students agree on?**

A. Game shows are the best.　　B. Documentaries should replace all other programs.  
C. TV content is important.　　D. Screen time should be zero.

> 📝 答案：**C**（"All three students agree on one thing: the content of TV programs matters."）

---

### 综合运用：表达观点

用以下句型写2-3句话，表达你对电视节目的看法：

**可用句型：**
- I prefer ... to ...
- I find ... (adj.)
- I would like to ...
- I think/believe that ...
- I hope that ...

> 📝 **参考答案**：  
> I prefer sports programs to soap operas because I find them more exciting and inspiring. I would like to watch more documentaries about Chinese athletes. I hope that TV stations will produce more educational programs for young people.

---

## 💡 易错点

### 观点类阅读题技巧

1. **确认说话人**：文章有三个人，每道题先确定是"谁的观点"
2. **区分事实与观点**：事实题找原文，观点题找关键词（think/believe/prefer/find）
3. **推理题**：答案需要从多句信息综合推断

> 🏐 **排球类比**：读多人观点文章就像看多点进攻——每个队员（每个人）都在不同位置（段落）出现，你要清楚每个人的站位（观点）。

---

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**书面表达（中考常考话题）：**  
你的学校正在讨论是否应该允许学生在宿舍看电视。请用英语写一段话（5句左右），表达你的看法，并给出理由。

> 📝 **参考答案**：  
> I think students should be allowed to watch TV in the dorms, but only for a limited time. First, watching educational programs like documentaries and news can help us learn more about the world. Second, after a tiring volleyball practice, watching a funny sitcom helps us relax. However, I hope that students will choose programs wisely and not waste time on meaningless shows. In conclusion, watching TV is fine as long as we do it in moderation.
