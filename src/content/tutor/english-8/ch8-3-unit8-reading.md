## 📚 核心内容

### 📖 阅读短文

**Eating Right for Sports**

Do you want to be a better athlete? What you eat is just as important as how you train. Here is some advice for young sports players.

**Before exercise:** Eat a light meal about two hours before you play. Don't eat too much, or you may feel sick. Good choices include bananas, whole-grain bread, and yogurt. These foods give you energy slowly and help you play better.

**During exercise:** Drink water regularly. Don't wait until you feel very thirsty. Sports drinks are OK, but be careful — many have too much sugar. The best drink is plain water.

**After exercise:** After a hard volleyball practice, your body needs protein to repair your muscles. Eat foods like eggs, chicken, milk, or beans. Don't forget vegetables too! Add some lettuce, tomatoes, or other vegetables to your meal.

**A simple post-game recipe:** Here is a quick and easy chicken sandwich.

*Ingredients:* 2 slices of whole-grain bread, cooked chicken, lettuce, one tomato, a little honey mustard

*Steps:*  
First, slice the tomato thinly. Next, put the chicken on one slice of bread. Then, add the lettuce and tomato slices. Finally, pour a little honey mustard on top and put the other slice of bread on. Your sandwich is ready!

Remember: eat well, train hard, and play your best!

### 🔍 阅读理解题

**1. When should you eat before exercise?**  
A. Right before you play  
B. About two hours before  
C. After you play  
D. During the game

**答案：B**  
解析：第二段说 "Eat a light meal about two hours before you play."

**2. What is the BEST drink during exercise, according to the passage?**  
A. Sports drinks  
B. Juice  
C. Milk  
D. Plain water

**答案：D**  
解析：第三段说 "The best drink is plain water."

**3. Why should you eat protein after exercise?**  
A. To get energy quickly  
B. To repair your muscles  
C. To lose weight  
D. To feel less thirsty

**答案：B**  
解析：第四段说 "your body needs protein to repair your muscles."

**4. How many steps are there in the chicken sandwich recipe?**  
A. Three  
B. Four  
C. Five  
D. Six

**答案：B**  
解析：食谱中有 First, Next, Then, Finally 四步。

### 🔍 词义猜测与语言点

**1. 文中的祈使句（命令/建议）：**
- **Eat** a light meal about two hours before you play.（建议）
- **Don't** eat too much.（否定建议）
- **Drink** water regularly.（建议）
- **Don't** wait until you feel very thirsty.（否定建议）
- **Don't** forget vegetables too!（提醒）

**2. 高频短语：**
- just as important as 和……一样重要
- be careful 小心、注意
- be ready 准备好了

**3. 数量词回顾：**
- 2 slices of bread（两片面包——slice + 可数名词）
- a little honey mustard（少量蜂蜜芥末——a little + 不可数名词）
- some protein（一些蛋白质——some + 不可数名词）

### ✍️ 综合运用：写一写

**任务：** 为排球训练后的加餐，写一个简单的食谱（3-5步）

**格式要求：**
- 列出 Ingredients（原料）
- 用 First/Next/Then/Finally 写步骤
- 使用祈使句（动词开头）

**参考答案：**

*Simple Banana Yogurt Bowl*

*Ingredients:* 2 bananas, 1 cup of yogurt, 2 spoons of honey

*Steps:*  
First, peel the bananas and cut them into small pieces.  
Next, put the banana pieces into a bowl.  
Then, pour the yogurt over the bananas.  
Finally, add two spoons of honey on top. Enjoy!

## 💡 易错点

### ❌ 错误1：just as... as 结构
- ✅ What you eat is **just as important as** how you train.
- 这是"同级比较"：as + 形容词 + as（和……一样……）
- just 起强调作用，等同 equally important

### ❌ 错误2：建议的祈使句 vs. 命令的祈使句
- 语气上，文中的祈使句是"建议"而不是"命令"
- 通过上下文区分：健康文章里的都是 advice（建议）
- 加 please 或 should 会更礼貌

### ❌ 错误3：repair vs. fix vs. heal
- repair 修复（物品、肌肉等）→ repair muscles
- fix 修理（物品）→ fix a bike
- heal 愈合（伤口）→ heal a wound

**书面表达（河南中考写作风格）：**

你的英国笔友 Tom 问你，运动员应该怎么饮食。请给他写一封短邮件（3-5句话），介绍运动前后的饮食建议。

**参考范文：**
```
Hi Tom,

Here are some tips about eating for sports.

Before exercise, eat a light meal about two hours before you play.
Good choices are bananas and yogurt. Don't eat too much!

After exercise, eat foods with protein like eggs or chicken.
These help repair your muscles after a hard game.

Remember: eat well, train hard, and you'll play your best!

Your friend,
[Name]
```
