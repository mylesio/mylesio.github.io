## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

排球赛季结束，老师推荐了几个郑州好去处！看看这篇"城市最佳推荐"，学最高级的真实应用，还要挑战综合理解题！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

> **The Best Places in Zhengzhou**
>
> Zhengzhou, the capital of Henan Province, has many wonderful places to visit and enjoy. Here are our top recommendations.
>
> **The best museum: Henan Museum**  
> Henan Museum is the oldest and most famous museum in our province. It has the largest collection of ancient Chinese artifacts in central China. If you are interested in history, this is the most educational place you will ever visit. The ticket is only 80 yuan, making it one of the most affordable museums of its kind.
>
> **The most exciting sport: watching a volleyball match**  
> If you want to see some of the best athletes in action, visit the Zhongzhou Volleyball Arena. The atmosphere there is the most thrilling of all the sports events we surveyed. Students get a 50% discount, so it's also one of the cheapest entertainment options for young people.
>
> **The best food street: Jiankang Road**  
> Jiankang Road food street is the longest food street in Zhengzhou, with over 200 restaurants and food stalls. The most popular dish is hu la tang (pepper soup), which locals say is the most delicious breakfast of all. It is also the nearest food street to the Zhengzhou railway station.
>
> **The most comfortable place to relax: Greenland Central Park**  
> Greenland Central Park is the largest park in the city center. On weekends, it is the most crowded place in town, but it is also the most peaceful — once you are deep inside, you can't hear the city noise at all.

---

### 阅读理解

**1. Which place is recommended for people interested in history?**

A. Jiankang Road　　B. Greenland Central Park  
C. Henan Museum　　D. Zhongzhou Volleyball Arena

> 📝 答案：**C**（"If you are interested in history, this is the most educational place"）

**2. How much would a student pay to watch a volleyball match if the original price is 100 yuan?**

A. 80 yuan　　B. 60 yuan　　C. 50 yuan　　D. 40 yuan

> 📝 答案：**C**（"Students get a 50% discount" → 100 × 50% = 50）

**3. What does "the most crowded" mean in the last paragraph?**

A. The most beautiful.　　B. The most popular with visitors.  
C. The most expensive.　　D. The fullest of people.

> 📝 答案：**D**（crowded = 拥挤的，充满人的）

**4. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as a reason to visit Jiankang Road?**

A. It's the longest food street.　　B. It has over 200 restaurants.  
C. It's close to the railway station.　　D. It has the best movie theater.

> 📝 答案：**D**（文章没有提到电影院）

### 综合运用：最高级改写

将下面的句子改为最高级句型：

1. Henan Museum has more artifacts than any other museum in central China.  
→ Henan Museum has ________ artifacts ________ all museums in central China.

2. No food street in Zhengzhou is longer than Jiankang Road.  
→ Jiankang Road is ________ food street ________ Zhengzhou.

> 📝 答案：
> 1. the most; of  
> 2. the longest; in

## 💡 易错点

### "最高级 vs 比较级" 陷阱

阅读中常用比较级来暗示最高级含义：

| 原句 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| No other player is taller than her. | = She is the tallest player. |
| She plays better than all others. | = She plays the best. |

考题常把这两种表达互换考察！

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**完形填空（节选）：**

> Our school canteen had a "best dish" survey last week. Students voted for their (1)________ (favorite) dish. The results were interesting. The most (2)________ dish was fried rice, chosen by 35% of students. The (3)________ popular dish was spicy noodles. The school chef was very happy to hear the (4)________.

1. favorite / most favorite → **favorite**（已是最高级含义，不再加 most）
2. popular ✅ 
3. second most → **second most**（第二受欢迎）
4. results ✅

> 📝 **考点**：favorite 本身表示"最喜欢的"，不需要再加 most；second most popular 是固定表达。
