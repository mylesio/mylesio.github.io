## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

你们省锦赛打到第五局，对手领先 10 分——你们放弃了吗？没有！这就是愚公精神。今天读一篇关于愚公的英语短文，练中考阅读技巧：**找主旨、猜词义、推理判断**，三大题型全覆盖！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 阅读短文

**The Spirit of Yugong**

Long ago in China, there was an old man called Yugong. He was ninety years old and lived with his family near two large mountains. These mountains blocked the road, and it was very difficult for the villagers to go in and out.

One day, Yugong decided to move the mountains. He called his sons and grandsons and began to dig. They carried the earth and stones to the sea. It was very hard work. A **wise** man named Zhisou laughed at Yugong and said, "You are so **silly**! You are old and weak. How can you move such big mountains?"

Yugong replied calmly, "When I die, my sons will continue. When they die, their sons will continue. **Generation after generation**, our family will keep working. But the mountains cannot grow bigger. One day, we will surely move them."

The gods in heaven **were moved by** Yugong's spirit. They sent two strong gods to carry the mountains away. From that day on, there were no more mountains blocking the road.

This old Chinese story **reminds** us of an important truth: if you never give up, even the most difficult task can be done. This is the spirit of Yugong — and it is still **alive** today in the hearts of the Chinese people.

### 中考题型训练

**A. 判断正误（T/F）**

1. Yugong was seventy years old when he decided to move the mountains. ___
2. Yugong and his family carried the earth and stones to the sea. ___
3. Zhisou agreed with Yugong's plan and helped him. ___
4. In the end, the gods helped Yugong by carrying the mountains away. ___

**答案：** 1. F（ninety）　2. T　3. F（laughed at him）　4. T

---

**B. 单项选择**

1. Why did Yugong want to move the mountains?

   A. Because he wanted to show his strength.  
   B. Because the mountains blocked the road for the villagers.  
   C. Because the gods told him to.  
   D. Because his sons asked him to.

   > **答案：B**（第一段：the mountains blocked the road）

2. What does "generation after generation" mean in the third paragraph?

   A. one person after another  
   B. one mountain after another  
   C. each generation following the last  
   D. a very long time ago  

   > **答案：C**（一代接一代，表示持续传承）

3. The gods moved the mountains because ________.

   A. Yugong was very old and weak  
   B. the villagers asked them to help  
   C. they were moved by Yugong's spirit  
   D. the mountains were too small to matter  

   > **答案：C**（第四段：were moved by Yugong's spirit）

---

**C. 简答题**

1. How did Yugong answer Zhisou's question?

   > **参考答案**：Yugong said that when he died, his sons and grandsons would continue the work. The mountains could not grow bigger, so one day they would be moved.

2. What is the main message of the story?

   > **参考答案**：If you never give up and keep working hard, even the most difficult task can be done.

## 💡 易错点

### 被动语态 were moved by

"The gods **were moved by** Yugong's spirit."

- **be moved by** = 被…感动（固定搭配）
- 注意：move 有两个意思：①移动 ②感动
- I was deeply **moved** by the story. ✅（我被故事深深感动了）
- Don't confuse: The mountains were **moved**（被移走）vs The gods were **moved by**（被感动）

### generation 的用法

```
generation = 一代人（约30年）
the next generation = 下一代
generation after generation = 一代又一代（不加 's'）
for generations = 几代人以来
```

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**完形填空（节选）：**

Yugong __(1)__ to move the two mountains. His neighbors thought he was __(2)__. But Yugong didn't __(3)__ up. He believed his family would __(4)__ the work for generations. Finally, the gods were __(5)__ by his spirit and helped him.

| 空 | 选项 | 答案 |
|----|------|------|
| (1) | A.wants B.decided C.is deciding D.decides | B |
| (2) | A.wise B.kind C.silly D.brave | C |
| (3) | A.take B.pick C.get D.give | D |
| (4) | A.continue B.stop C.forget D.change | A |
| (5) | A.moved B.helped C.seen D.stopped | A |

---

**书面表达（50词）：**

The spirit of Yugong inspires you. Write a short paragraph about **a time when you didn't give up** (in volleyball or another activity).

> **参考答案**：
> Last year, our volleyball team lost the first two sets in the final match. Everyone felt disappointed, but we didn't give up. We remembered the spirit of Yugong — keep going, generation after generation. We came back and won the last three sets. That experience taught me that nothing is impossible if you never stop trying.
