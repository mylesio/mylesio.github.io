## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

教练请你帮忙整理装备，怎么开口最礼貌？队友问你能不能换一下发球位置，你怎么回应？今天学的 **Could you please...?** 就是"礼貌版"请求神器！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 一、could 的用法总览

| 用法 | 语气 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| can 的过去式 | 过去能力 | I **could** run fast when I was young. |
| 礼貌请求 | 委婉语气 | **Could** you help me? |
| 礼貌提议 | 建议 | You **could** try harder. |

> 💡 **Could you please...?** 比 **Can you...?** 更礼貌！中考中请求别人做事，优先用 could。

---

### 二、礼貌请求的四种句型

| 句型 | 例句 | 语气 |
|------|------|------|
| Could you (please) + V? | Could you please clean your room? | 礼貌 |
| Can you + V? | Can you help me? | 普通 |
| Would you mind + Ving? | Would you mind sweeping the floor? | 更客气 |
| Will you + V? | Will you do the dishes? | 普通请求 |

---

### 三、Could you please...? 的肯定/否定回应

```
请求：Could you please do the dishes?

✅ 肯定回答（同意）：
- Sure. / Of course. / No problem. / I'd be happy to.

✅ 否定回答（拒绝，要给理由）：
- Sorry, I can't. I have to finish my homework first.
- I'm afraid I can't. I'm busy right now.
```

> ❌ 不要光说 "No." 或 "I can't."——没有理由的拒绝在英语中不礼貌！

---

### 四、Could I please...? vs. Could you please...?

| 句子 | 谁在请求 | 意思 |
|------|----------|------|
| Could **I** please use your phone? | 我请求对方允许我做 | 我能用你的手机吗？ |
| Could **you** please wash the car? | 我请求对方帮我做 | 你能帮我洗车吗？ |

---

### 五、Would you mind + Ving?（进阶用法）

```
Would you mind closing the window?  你介意关一下窗吗？

✅ 同意（= 我不介意，愿意做）：
- Not at all. / Of course not. / No problem.

✅ 拒绝（= 我介意，不愿意做）：
- I'm sorry, but I'm cold. / I'm afraid I can't.
```

> ⚠️ 注意：回答 "Would you mind...?" 时，**Not at all = 愿意做**（我不介意），不要搞反！

---

### 六、家务情景对话模板

```
A: Could you please sweep the floor?
B: Sure, no problem.

A: Would you mind doing the laundry?
B: Not at all. I'll do it right away.

A: Could I please go out tonight?
B: Sorry, you need to finish your homework first.
```

---

## 💡 易错点

1. **could 后接动词原形**  
   ✅ Could you **help** me?  ❌ Could you **to help** me?

2. **Would you mind + V-ing**（不是动词原形！）  
   ✅ Would you mind **cleaning**?  ❌ Would you mind **clean**?

3. **回答 "Would you mind...?" 的陷阱**  
   - Not at all / Of course not = **同意做**（我不介意）  
   - Yes, I do mind = **不同意做**（我介意）  
   → 中考常考 "Not at all" 选项

4. **Could you please VS. Could I please**  
   - you = 你帮我做  
   - I = 我获得允许做  
   这两个意思完全不同！

5. **拒绝请求必须给理由**，否则显得失礼  
   ✅ Sorry, I can't. I'm busy.  ❌ No. （太唐突）
