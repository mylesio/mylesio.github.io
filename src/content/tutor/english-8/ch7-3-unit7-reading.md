## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

2050年的排球比赛：机器人裁判不会犯错，机器人后勤员可以分析每一个战术！  
这是幻想吗？科学家们认为这一切可能真的会发生。  
今天我们来读一篇关于机器人未来的文章，练习中考阅读技巧！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 📖 阅读短文

**Robots in Our Future**

Do you have a robot at home? Probably not yet. But scientists predict that in the next 50 years, robots will become part of our everyday lives.

Today, robots are already used in factories to make cars and other products. They work faster and make fewer mistakes than humans. In the future, robots will probably do many more jobs. They will help doctors in hospitals, teach students in classrooms, and even cook meals at home.

Some people worry that robots will take away human jobs. That is true for some jobs. For example, robots will do dangerous jobs like fighting fires or working in deep oceans. But many experts say that robots will also create new jobs that don't exist today.

What about personal robots? Scientists say that by 2050, many families will have a robot at home. These robots will clean the house, take care of old people, and even play sports with children. Imagine having a robot volleyball partner to practice with!

However, there are some things robots will never replace. Robots won't understand human feelings deeply. They won't create art or music with real emotion. And they will never truly replace the joy of real human friendship.

The future will have more robots, but humans will always be important. The question is: how do we work together with robots?

---

### 🔍 阅读理解题

**1. Where are robots already used today?**  
A. In schools  
B. In homes  
C. In factories  
D. In hospitals only

**答案：C**  
解析：第二段说 "robots are already used in factories to make cars and other products."

**2. According to the passage, which of the following jobs will robots do?**  
A. Fighting fires  
B. Writing novels  
C. Making friends  
D. Composing music

**答案：A**  
解析：第三段 "robots will do dangerous jobs like fighting fires or working in deep oceans."

**3. What does the word "replace" mean in the last two paragraphs?**  
A. 替代  
B. 创造  
C. 发现  
D. 修理

**答案：A**  
解析：通过上下文 "robots will never replace human feelings" 可以推断 replace = 替代、取代。

**4. What is the main idea of this passage?**  
A. Robots are dangerous to humans.  
B. Robots will replace all human workers.  
C. Robots will play an important role but humans remain important.  
D. We should stop making robots.

**答案：C**  
解析：全文既讲了机器人的未来作用，又强调了人类不可替代的价值，结尾问"如何合作"，主旨是C。

### 🔍 词义猜测练习

**根据上下文，猜测下列词汇的意思：**

1. **predict** (paragraph 1): "scientists **predict** that robots will become part of our lives"  
   → 意思是：**预测、预言**（science + 将来会发生的事）

2. **experts** (paragraph 3): "many **experts** say that robots will create new jobs"  
   → 意思是：**专家**（提出权威意见的人）

3. **emotion** (paragraph 5): "create art or music with real **emotion**"  
   → 意思是：**情感、感情**（艺术需要的内在感受）

---

### ✍️ 综合运用

**用 will / won't 完成下列句子（根据短文信息）：**

1. Robots _______ do dangerous jobs like fighting fires. → **will**
2. Robots _______ understand human feelings deeply. → **won't**
3. By 2050, many families _______ have a robot at home. → **will**
4. Robots _______ truly replace real human friendship. → **won't**

---

## 💡 易错点

### ❌ 错误1：there are/will be 与 they have/will have 混用
- ✅ **There will be** more robots in 2050.（用 there will be 表示存在）
- ✅ Many families **will have** robots.（用 have 表示拥有）
- ❌ There will have more robots.（错误！there 不能接 have）

### ❌ 错误2：fewer mistakes vs. less mistakes
- ✅ robots make **fewer** mistakes（mistake 是可数名词，用 fewer）
- ❌ robots make **less** mistakes

### ❌ 错误3：take away jobs（固定搭配）
- take away = 带走、剥夺
- robots will take away human jobs = 机器人会夺走人类工作
- ✅ Robots will **take away** some jobs.
- ❌ Robots will **take** some jobs **away from** humans.（也可以，但结构略复杂）
