## 🏐 热身（Warm-up）

排球场上，教练说："如果你们努力练习，**将会**赢得比赛！"  
这种对未来的预测和承诺，用 **will** 来表达。  
今天搞懂 will 将来时和 there will be 结构——中考年年考！

---

## 📚 核心内容

### 一、will 的基本结构

```
主语 + will + 动词原形
```

**重要：will 不随主语变化，永远是 will！**

| 主语 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I | I will | I **will** be a scientist. |
| You | You will | You **will** win! |
| He/She | He/She will | She **will** travel to space. |
| We/They | We/They will | They **will** have robots. |

**缩写：** will → 'll
- I'll = I will
- He'll = He will
- They'll = They will

---

### 二、否定句

```
主语 + will + NOT + 动词原形
will not = won't
```

- People **won't** use paper books in the future.
- Robots **won't** replace all humans.
- I **won't** give up my dream!

---

### 三、疑问句

**一般疑问句：**
```
Will + 主语 + 动词原形?
→ Yes, 主语 + will. / No, 主语 + won't.
```

- **Will** there be robots in every home?  
  → Yes, there will. / No, there won't.

- **Will** people live on Mars?  
  → Yes, they will. / No, they won't.

**特殊疑问句：**
```
疑问词 + will + 主语 + 动词原形?
```

- **What** will robots do in the future?  
  → They will help people with housework.

- **Where** will people live in 100 years?  
  → They might live on other planets.

---

### 四、there will be 结构（重点！）

```
There will be + 名词（单/复数/不可数）
否定：There won't be + 名词
疑问：Will there be + 名词?
```

**There will be vs. There is/are：**
- There **are** many cars now.（现在有很多车）
- There **will be** even more cars in 2050.（2050年将有更多车）

**例句：**
- There **will be** more robots in the future.（将来会有更多机器人）
- There **won't be** any paper money.（将不会有纸币）
- **Will** there **be** schools in 2100?（2100年还会有学校吗？）

> ⚠️ **注意：there will be 后面不能再用 is/are！**
> - ✅ There **will be** a big change.
> - ❌ There **will is** a big change.

---

### 五、will vs. be going to 对比（考试必备）

| 特征 | will | be going to |
|------|------|-------------|
| **含义** | 临时决定/预测/承诺 | 事先计划好的 |
| **例句1** | I'll help you!（临时决定） | I'm going to help you tomorrow.（早就计划了） |
| **例句2** | It will rain.（根据天气预测） | Look at those clouds! It's going to rain.（有明显依据） |
| **常搭配** | probably, I think, maybe | tomorrow, next week, this summer |

**中考常考区分情景：**
- 看到窗外乌云密布 → **It's going to rain.**（有依据的预测 → be going to）
- 没有依据，只是猜测 → **It will probably rain.**（纯预测 → will）
- 妈妈说"我现在帮你" → **I'll help you.**（临时决定 → will）

---

### 六、常用时间状语（配合 will）

| 时间状语 | 含义 |
|----------|------|
| in the future | 将来 |
| in + 年份（in 2050） | 在2050年 |
| in 100 years | 100年后 |
| someday | 某天 |
| soon | 不久 |
| one day | 有一天 |

---

## 💡 易错点

### ❌ 错误1：will 后面不加 to
- ✅ She will **be** a doctor.
- ❌ She will **to be** a doctor.

### ❌ 错误2：will 后面用动词原形，不加 -s/-ed
- ✅ He **will go** there tomorrow.
- ❌ He **will goes** there tomorrow.

### ❌ 错误3：there will be 后面不用 is/are
- ✅ **There will be** fewer trees.
- ❌ **There will is** fewer trees.

### ❌ 错误4：won't = will not（缩写要记住）
- ✅ I **won't** go.（= will not）
- 注意：won't 不是 want 的缩写！

### ❌ 错误5：will 没有人称变化
- ✅ She **will** come.（不是 wills）
- ✅ I **will** come.（不是 shall，现代英语中 will 更常用）

## 🎯 河南中考真题模拟

**题目1（按要求改句）：**  
People will use robots to do housework in the future.（改一般疑问句）  
→ **Will people use robots to do housework in the future?**  
→ 简短回答：**Yes, they will. / No, they won't.**

**题目2（翻译）：**  
50年后将会有更少的工厂。  
→ **There will be fewer factories in 50 years.**

**题目3（选择）：**  
— Look! It's very cloudy outside.  
— Yes, I think it ______ rain this afternoon.  
A. will　　B. is going to　　C. was　　D. goes  
→ **答案：B（is going to）**  
解析：看到窗外乌云密布，有具体依据，用 be going to 表示对近期将来的有把握预测。
